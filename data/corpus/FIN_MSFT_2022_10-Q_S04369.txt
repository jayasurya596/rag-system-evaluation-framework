Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $248.95 billion, compared to $216.48 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $43.62 billion, compared to $51.00 billion. Operating income increased to $54.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.44 billion at the end of the reporting period.
