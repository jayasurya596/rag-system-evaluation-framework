For the fiscal year, total net sales were $310.39 billion, compared to $269.90 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $52.41 billion, compared to $66.07 billion. Operating income increased to $90.58 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.84 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.31 billion at the end of the reporting period.
