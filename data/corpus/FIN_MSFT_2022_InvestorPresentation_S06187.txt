Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $232.35 billion, compared to $202.04 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $49.56 billion, compared to $43.71 billion. Operating income increased to $59.83 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.89 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $91.00 billion at the end of the reporting period.
