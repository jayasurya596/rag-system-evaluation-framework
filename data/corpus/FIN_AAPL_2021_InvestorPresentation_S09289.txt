Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $392.83 billion, compared to $363.73 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $72.26 billion, compared to $87.37 billion. Operating income increased to $99.12 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $41.92 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $109.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
