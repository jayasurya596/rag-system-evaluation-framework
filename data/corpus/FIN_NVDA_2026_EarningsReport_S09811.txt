For the fiscal year, total net sales were $437.02 billion, compared to $273.14 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $90.96 billion, compared to $43.92 billion. Operating income increased to $112.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.93 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $158.93 billion at the end of the reporting period.
