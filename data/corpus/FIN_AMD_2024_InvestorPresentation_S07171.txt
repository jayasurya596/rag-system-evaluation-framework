Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $32.41 billion, compared to $27.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.46 billion, compared to $6.37 billion. Operating income increased to $9.00 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.19 billion at the end of the reporting period.
