Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $389.14 billion, compared to $360.31 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $94.68 billion, compared to $63.01 billion. Operating income increased to $104.23 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.14 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $85.51 billion at the end of the reporting period.
