Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $210.72 billion, compared to $183.23 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $36.48 billion, compared to $43.58 billion. Operating income increased to $44.09 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $18.08 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $25.61 billion at the end of the reporting period.
