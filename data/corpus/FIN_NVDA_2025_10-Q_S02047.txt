Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $268.92 billion, compared to $168.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $55.59 billion, compared to $36.93 billion. Operating income increased to $65.80 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.35 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.43 billion at the end of the reporting period.
