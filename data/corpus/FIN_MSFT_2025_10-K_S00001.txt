Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $351.14 billion, compared to $305.34 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $75.49 billion, compared to $62.44 billion. Operating income increased to $77.97 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $120.38 billion at the end of the reporting period.
