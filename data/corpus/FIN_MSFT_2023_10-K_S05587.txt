Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $285.08 billion, compared to $247.90 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $49.28 billion, compared to $55.61 billion. Operating income increased to $57.99 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.67 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $80.30 billion at the end of the reporting period.
