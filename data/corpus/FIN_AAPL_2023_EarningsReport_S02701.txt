Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $461.42 billion, compared to $427.24 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $101.18 billion, compared to $85.23 billion. Operating income increased to $114.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $52.51 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $57.06 billion at the end of the reporting period.
