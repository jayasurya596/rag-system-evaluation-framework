Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $167.87 billion, compared to $104.92 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $28.66 billion, compared to $23.20 billion. Operating income increased to $44.04 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $13.90 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $51.53 billion at the end of the reporting period.
