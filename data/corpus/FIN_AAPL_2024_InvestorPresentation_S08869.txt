Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $480.69 billion, compared to $445.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $98.17 billion, compared to $97.06 billion. Operating income increased to $114.48 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.86 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $134.95 billion at the end of the reporting period.
