Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $534.79 billion, compared to $495.17 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $82.92 billion, compared to $93.36 billion. Operating income increased to $136.09 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $57.29 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $53.71 billion at the end of the reporting period.
