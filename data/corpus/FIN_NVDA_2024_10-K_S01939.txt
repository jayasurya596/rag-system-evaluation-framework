Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $165.43 billion, compared to $103.39 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $31.98 billion, compared to $22.35 billion. Operating income increased to $39.51 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $15.92 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $29.11 billion at the end of the reporting period.
