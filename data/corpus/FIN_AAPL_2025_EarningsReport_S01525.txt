Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $510.16 billion, compared to $472.37 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $107.41 billion, compared to $93.39 billion. Operating income increased to $149.74 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $60.98 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $153.41 billion at the end of the reporting period.
