Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $64.53 billion, compared to $40.33 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $13.75 billion, compared to $6.19 billion. Operating income increased to $15.11 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $5.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $21.23 billion at the end of the reporting period.
