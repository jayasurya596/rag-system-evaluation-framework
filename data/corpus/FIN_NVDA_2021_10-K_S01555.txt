Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $39.29 billion, compared to $24.56 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $9.29 billion, compared to $5.96 billion. Operating income increased to $9.47 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.21 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.90 billion at the end of the reporting period.
