Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $493.46 billion, compared to $440.58 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $111.05 billion, compared to $95.71 billion. Operating income increased to $106.16 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.31 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $133.99 billion at the end of the reporting period.
