Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $549.47 billion, compared to $508.77 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $83.62 billion, compared to $89.33 billion. Operating income increased to $147.08 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $63.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $88.94 billion at the end of the reporting period.
