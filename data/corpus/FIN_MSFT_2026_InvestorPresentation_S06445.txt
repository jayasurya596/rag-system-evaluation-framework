Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $395.71 billion, compared to $344.10 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $82.29 billion, compared to $68.65 billion. Operating income increased to $87.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.38 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $157.73 billion at the end of the reporting period.
