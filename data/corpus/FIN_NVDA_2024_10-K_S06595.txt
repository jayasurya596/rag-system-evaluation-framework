Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $164.20 billion, compared to $102.63 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $40.12 billion, compared to $24.37 billion. Operating income increased to $45.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $13.48 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $53.85 billion at the end of the reporting period.
