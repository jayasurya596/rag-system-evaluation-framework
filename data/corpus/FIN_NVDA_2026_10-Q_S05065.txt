For the fiscal year, total net sales were $421.04 billion, compared to $263.15 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $95.33 billion, compared to $51.76 billion. Operating income increased to $113.62 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.23 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $142.57 billion at the end of the reporting period.
