Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $370.24 billion, compared to $342.82 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $65.71 billion, compared to $83.07 billion. Operating income increased to $83.24 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.85 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
