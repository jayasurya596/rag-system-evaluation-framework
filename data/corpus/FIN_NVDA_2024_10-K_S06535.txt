Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $162.83 billion, compared to $101.77 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $38.42 billion, compared to $16.66 billion. Operating income increased to $44.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $18.75 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $53.81 billion at the end of the reporting period.
