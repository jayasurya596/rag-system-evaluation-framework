For the fiscal year, total net sales were $38.48 billion, compared to $32.06 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.84 billion, compared to $6.36 billion. Operating income increased to $9.37 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.20 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.95 billion at the end of the reporting period.
