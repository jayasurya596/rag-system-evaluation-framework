Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $37.16 billion, compared to $30.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $8.68 billion, compared to $7.69 billion. Operating income increased to $10.81 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.80 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
