Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $535.32 billion, compared to $495.67 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $115.03 billion, compared to $116.62 billion. Operating income increased to $135.04 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $55.47 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $157.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
