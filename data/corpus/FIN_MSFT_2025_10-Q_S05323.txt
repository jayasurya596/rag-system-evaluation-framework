Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $363.61 billion, compared to $316.18 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $56.81 billion, compared to $65.45 billion. Operating income increased to $85.04 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.01 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $137.68 billion at the end of the reporting period.
