For the fiscal year, total net sales were $313.00 billion, compared to $272.18 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $61.75 billion, compared to $64.94 billion. Operating income increased to $64.84 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.88 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $43.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
