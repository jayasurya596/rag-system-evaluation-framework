Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $494.49 billion, compared to $457.86 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $122.90 billion, compared to $88.85 billion. Operating income increased to $132.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.40 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $134.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
