Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $465.51 billion, compared to $431.03 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $113.73 billion, compared to $85.73 billion. Operating income increased to $112.83 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.03 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $48.38 billion at the end of the reporting period.
