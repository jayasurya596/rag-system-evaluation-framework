For the fiscal year, total net sales were $45.31 billion, compared to $37.76 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $10.83 billion, compared to $5.67 billion. Operating income increased to $11.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.96 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.52 billion at the end of the reporting period.
