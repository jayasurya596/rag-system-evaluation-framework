For the fiscal year, total net sales were $100.75 billion, compared to $62.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $20.85 billion, compared to $11.56 billion. Operating income increased to $26.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $9.35 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $26.18 billion at the end of the reporting period.
