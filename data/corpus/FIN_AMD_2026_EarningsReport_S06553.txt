For the fiscal year, total net sales were $46.80 billion, compared to $39.00 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $9.61 billion, compared to $8.76 billion. Operating income increased to $13.22 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.87 billion at the end of the reporting period.
