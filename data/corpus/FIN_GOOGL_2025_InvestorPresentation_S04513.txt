For the fiscal year, total net sales were $443.00 billion, compared to $395.53 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $104.93 billion, compared to $66.89 billion. Operating income increased to $105.25 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.01 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $61.70 billion at the end of the reporting period.
