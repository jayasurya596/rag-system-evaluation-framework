For the fiscal year, total net sales were $404.62 billion, compared to $252.89 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $61.79 billion, compared to $52.54 billion. Operating income increased to $107.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $67.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
