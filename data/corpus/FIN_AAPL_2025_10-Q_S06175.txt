Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $532.52 billion, compared to $493.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $132.26 billion, compared to $76.85 billion. Operating income increased to $106.96 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.41 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $147.13 billion at the end of the reporting period.
