For the fiscal year, total net sales were $407.75 billion, compared to $354.57 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $87.32 billion, compared to $79.50 billion. Operating income increased to $101.50 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.12 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $146.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
