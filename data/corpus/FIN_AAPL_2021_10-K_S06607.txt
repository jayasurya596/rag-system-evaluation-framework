Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $391.18 billion, compared to $362.20 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $60.51 billion, compared to $70.57 billion. Operating income increased to $89.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.48 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $140.13 billion at the end of the reporting period.
