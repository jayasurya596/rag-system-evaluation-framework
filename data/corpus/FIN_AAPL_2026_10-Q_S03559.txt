Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $572.08 billion, compared to $529.70 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $98.42 billion, compared to $119.68 billion. Operating income increased to $130.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $68.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $135.29 billion at the end of the reporting period.
