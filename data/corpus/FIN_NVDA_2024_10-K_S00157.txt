Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $166.61 billion, compared to $104.13 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $28.07 billion, compared to $17.08 billion. Operating income increased to $38.71 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $14.98 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $29.70 billion at the end of the reporting period.
