Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $35.47 billion, compared to $29.55 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.25 billion, compared to $5.71 billion. Operating income increased to $9.14 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.89 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
