For the fiscal year, total net sales were $399.84 billion, compared to $249.90 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $99.82 billion, compared to $55.09 billion. Operating income increased to $80.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.93 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $72.53 billion at the end of the reporting period.
