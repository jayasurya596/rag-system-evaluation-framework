Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $485.80 billion, compared to $449.82 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $92.02 billion, compared to $81.78 billion. Operating income increased to $115.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.28 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $128.03 billion at the end of the reporting period.
