Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $537.51 billion, compared to $497.70 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $133.51 billion, compared to $108.50 billion. Operating income increased to $142.24 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.46 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $88.72 billion at the end of the reporting period.
