Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $37.03 billion, compared to $30.86 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.53 billion, compared to $7.30 billion. Operating income increased to $8.79 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $12.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
