For the fiscal year, total net sales were $156.97 billion, compared to $98.10 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $24.17 billion, compared to $18.95 billion. Operating income increased to $36.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $14.13 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $20.00 billion at the end of the reporting period.
