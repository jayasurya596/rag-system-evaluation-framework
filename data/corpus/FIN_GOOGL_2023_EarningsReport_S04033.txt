Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $327.75 billion, compared to $292.63 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $65.40 billion, compared to $59.92 billion. Operating income increased to $67.84 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.44 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $55.49 billion at the end of the reporting period.
