Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $503.98 billion, compared to $466.65 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $82.66 billion, compared to $84.89 billion. Operating income increased to $128.37 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $51.33 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $138.46 billion at the end of the reporting period.
