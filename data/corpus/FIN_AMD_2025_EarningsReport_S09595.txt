For the fiscal year, total net sales were $36.73 billion, compared to $30.61 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.97 billion, compared to $4.78 billion. Operating income increased to $10.20 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.41 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $8.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
