Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $43.47 billion, compared to $36.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.15 billion, compared to $8.12 billion. Operating income increased to $10.85 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.87 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $13.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
