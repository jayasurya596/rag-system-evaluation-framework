Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $366.86 billion, compared to $339.68 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $61.18 billion, compared to $65.16 billion. Operating income increased to $88.39 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.67 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $98.40 billion at the end of the reporting period.
