Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $313.47 billion, compared to $279.88 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $64.75 billion, compared to $60.44 billion. Operating income increased to $67.40 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.97 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $48.17 billion at the end of the reporting period.
