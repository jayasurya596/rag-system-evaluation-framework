For the fiscal year, total net sales were $45.57 billion, compared to $37.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.98 billion, compared to $9.20 billion. Operating income increased to $9.72 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $5.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $12.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
