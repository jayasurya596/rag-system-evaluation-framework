Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $351.39 billion, compared to $305.56 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $78.68 billion, compared to $64.93 billion. Operating income increased to $72.88 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.19 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $136.51 billion at the end of the reporting period.
