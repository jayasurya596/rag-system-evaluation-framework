Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $226.98 billion, compared to $197.37 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $37.21 billion, compared to $38.63 billion. Operating income increased to $62.91 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.20 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $32.11 billion at the end of the reporting period.
