For the fiscal year, total net sales were $435.20 billion, compared to $378.43 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $82.44 billion, compared to $94.11 billion. Operating income increased to $109.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.57 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $135.38 billion at the end of the reporting period.
