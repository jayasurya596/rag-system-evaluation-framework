Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $328.61 billion, compared to $293.40 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $50.99 billion, compared to $46.34 billion. Operating income increased to $81.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.11 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $49.59 billion at the end of the reporting period.
