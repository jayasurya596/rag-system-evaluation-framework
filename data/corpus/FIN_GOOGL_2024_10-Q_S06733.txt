Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $367.27 billion, compared to $327.92 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $72.19 billion, compared to $57.78 billion. Operating income increased to $93.37 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $110.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
