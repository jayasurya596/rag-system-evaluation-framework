Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $208.74 billion, compared to $181.51 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $39.70 billion, compared to $28.28 billion. Operating income increased to $49.31 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $24.74 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $27.11 billion at the end of the reporting period.
