Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $529.36 billion, compared to $490.15 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $82.12 billion, compared to $84.85 billion. Operating income increased to $145.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $53.60 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $145.73 billion at the end of the reporting period.
