Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $361.68 billion, compared to $314.50 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $66.00 billion, compared to $61.57 billion. Operating income increased to $81.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $40.71 billion at the end of the reporting period.
