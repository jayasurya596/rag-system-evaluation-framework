For the fiscal year, total net sales were $419.39 billion, compared to $364.69 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $79.25 billion, compared to $85.32 billion. Operating income increased to $109.97 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $49.39 billion at the end of the reporting period.
