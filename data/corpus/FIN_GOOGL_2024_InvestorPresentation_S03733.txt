For the fiscal year, total net sales were $386.52 billion, compared to $345.11 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $89.70 billion, compared to $83.91 billion. Operating income increased to $93.11 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.29 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $82.50 billion at the end of the reporting period.
