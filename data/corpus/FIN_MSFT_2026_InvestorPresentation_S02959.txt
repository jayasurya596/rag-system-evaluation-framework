For the fiscal year, total net sales were $397.65 billion, compared to $345.79 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $87.83 billion, compared to $65.31 billion. Operating income increased to $114.33 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.55 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $61.29 billion at the end of the reporting period.
