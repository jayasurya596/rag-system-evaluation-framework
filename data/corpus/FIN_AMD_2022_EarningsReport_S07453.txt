Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $20.55 billion, compared to $17.12 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $5.12 billion, compared to $3.07 billion. Operating income increased to $5.62 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $1.68 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
