Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $227.74 billion, compared to $198.04 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $44.07 billion, compared to $37.82 billion. Operating income increased to $46.13 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $20.31 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $79.98 billion at the end of the reporting period.
