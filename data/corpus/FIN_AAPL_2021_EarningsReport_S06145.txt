Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $376.63 billion, compared to $348.73 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $67.17 billion, compared to $71.14 billion. Operating income increased to $105.33 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.64 billion at the end of the reporting period.
