For the fiscal year, total net sales were $528.73 billion, compared to $489.56 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $109.41 billion, compared to $98.21 billion. Operating income increased to $153.65 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $159.86 billion at the end of the reporting period.
