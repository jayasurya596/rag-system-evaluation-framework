For the fiscal year, total net sales were $157.40 billion, compared to $98.37 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $27.73 billion, compared to $15.77 billion. Operating income increased to $43.53 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $16.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $17.28 billion at the end of the reporting period.
