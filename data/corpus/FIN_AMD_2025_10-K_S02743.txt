Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $38.45 billion, compared to $32.04 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.70 billion, compared to $6.14 billion. Operating income increased to $9.79 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.65 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
