For the fiscal year, total net sales were $427.54 billion, compared to $371.77 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $77.10 billion, compared to $70.06 billion. Operating income increased to $92.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $94.52 billion at the end of the reporting period.
