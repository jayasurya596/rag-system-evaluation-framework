Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $160.06 billion, compared to $100.03 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $29.45 billion, compared to $23.34 billion. Operating income increased to $41.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $15.03 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $62.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
