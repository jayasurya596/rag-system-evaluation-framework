Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $211.64 billion, compared to $184.04 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $33.69 billion, compared to $32.22 billion. Operating income increased to $63.06 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $24.59 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $36.31 billion at the end of the reporting period.
