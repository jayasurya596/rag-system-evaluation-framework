Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $216.60 billion, compared to $188.35 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $35.84 billion, compared to $32.14 billion. Operating income increased to $64.49 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $17.81 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $63.11 billion at the end of the reporting period.
