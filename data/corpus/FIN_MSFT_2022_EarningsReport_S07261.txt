Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $235.67 billion, compared to $204.93 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $56.92 billion, compared to $48.45 billion. Operating income increased to $64.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $23.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $36.19 billion at the end of the reporting period.
