Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $17.25 billion, compared to $14.38 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $3.57 billion, compared to $3.35 billion. Operating income increased to $4.12 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $1.63 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.53 billion at the end of the reporting period.
