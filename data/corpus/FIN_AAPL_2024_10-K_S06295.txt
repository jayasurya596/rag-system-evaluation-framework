Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $483.72 billion, compared to $447.89 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $99.92 billion, compared to $111.29 billion. Operating income increased to $143.25 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.68 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $75.89 billion at the end of the reporting period.
