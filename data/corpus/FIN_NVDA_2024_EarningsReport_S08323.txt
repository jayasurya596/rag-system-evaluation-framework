Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $161.47 billion, compared to $100.92 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $24.34 billion, compared to $23.20 billion. Operating income increased to $41.13 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $13.63 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $46.46 billion at the end of the reporting period.
