Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $301.80 billion, compared to $262.44 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $57.76 billion, compared to $48.70 billion. Operating income increased to $75.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.20 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $43.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
