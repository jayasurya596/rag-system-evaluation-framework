For the fiscal year, total net sales were $430.63 billion, compared to $374.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $68.87 billion, compared to $57.72 billion. Operating income increased to $116.98 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.76 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $44.33 billion at the end of the reporting period.
