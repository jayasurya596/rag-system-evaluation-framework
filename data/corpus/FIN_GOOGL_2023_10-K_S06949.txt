Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $329.72 billion, compared to $294.39 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $77.20 billion, compared to $63.86 billion. Operating income increased to $77.30 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.31 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $127.28 billion at the end of the reporting period.
