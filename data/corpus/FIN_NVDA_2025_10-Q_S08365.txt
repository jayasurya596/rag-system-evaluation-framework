Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $252.24 billion, compared to $157.65 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $38.60 billion, compared to $27.32 billion. Operating income increased to $53.82 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.63 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $70.89 billion at the end of the reporting period.
