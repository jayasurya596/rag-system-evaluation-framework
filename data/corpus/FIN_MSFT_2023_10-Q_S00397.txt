Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $260.45 billion, compared to $226.47 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $57.14 billion, compared to $36.49 billion. Operating income increased to $63.81 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $42.38 billion at the end of the reporting period.
