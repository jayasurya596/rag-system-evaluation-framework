Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $202.16 billion, compared to $175.79 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $43.59 billion, compared to $42.41 billion. Operating income increased to $43.17 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $19.11 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $58.70 billion at the end of the reporting period.
