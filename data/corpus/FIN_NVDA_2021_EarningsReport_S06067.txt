For the fiscal year, total net sales were $40.54 billion, compared to $25.34 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $7.16 billion, compared to $3.88 billion. Operating income increased to $10.05 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.35 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $13.84 billion at the end of the reporting period.
