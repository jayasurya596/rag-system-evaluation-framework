Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $303.26 billion, compared to $270.77 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $62.79 billion, compared to $57.19 billion. Operating income increased to $81.02 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.93 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $54.57 billion at the end of the reporting period.
