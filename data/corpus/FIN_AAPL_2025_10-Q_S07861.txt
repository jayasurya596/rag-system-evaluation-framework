Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $512.33 billion, compared to $474.38 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $123.90 billion, compared to $93.44 billion. Operating income increased to $102.91 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $57.33 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $157.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
