For the fiscal year, total net sales were $396.58 billion, compared to $344.85 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $73.17 billion, compared to $57.64 billion. Operating income increased to $112.99 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.74 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $112.94 billion at the end of the reporting period.
