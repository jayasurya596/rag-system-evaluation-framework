Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $286.41 billion, compared to $255.72 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $53.01 billion, compared to $54.00 billion. Operating income increased to $70.75 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $24.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $75.20 billion at the end of the reporting period.
