Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $276.18 billion, compared to $246.59 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $51.60 billion, compared to $45.84 billion. Operating income increased to $62.39 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.86 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $85.02 billion at the end of the reporting period.
