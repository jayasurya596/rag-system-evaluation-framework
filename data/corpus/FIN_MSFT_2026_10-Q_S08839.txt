For the fiscal year, total net sales were $419.33 billion, compared to $364.64 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $88.24 billion, compared to $72.76 billion. Operating income increased to $86.39 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $98.57 billion at the end of the reporting period.
