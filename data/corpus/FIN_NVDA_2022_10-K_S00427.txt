Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $64.50 billion, compared to $40.31 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $11.81 billion, compared to $9.74 billion. Operating income increased to $18.03 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $5.33 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.34 billion at the end of the reporting period.
