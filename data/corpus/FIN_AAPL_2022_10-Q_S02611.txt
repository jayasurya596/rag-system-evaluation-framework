Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $422.56 billion, compared to $391.26 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $103.60 billion, compared to $94.04 billion. Operating income increased to $92.82 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $120.77 billion at the end of the reporting period.
