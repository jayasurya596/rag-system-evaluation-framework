For the fiscal year, total net sales were $409.96 billion, compared to $256.23 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $77.05 billion, compared to $60.03 billion. Operating income increased to $116.11 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.62 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $144.82 billion at the end of the reporting period.
