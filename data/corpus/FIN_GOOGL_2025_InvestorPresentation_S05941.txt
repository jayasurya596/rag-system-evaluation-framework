For the fiscal year, total net sales were $426.43 billion, compared to $380.74 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $102.56 billion, compared to $82.86 billion. Operating income increased to $96.72 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.20 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $126.08 billion at the end of the reporting period.
