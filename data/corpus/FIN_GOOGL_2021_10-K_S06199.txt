Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $281.63 billion, compared to $251.45 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $55.28 billion, compared to $49.42 billion. Operating income increased to $83.83 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.32 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $67.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
