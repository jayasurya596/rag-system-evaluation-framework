For the fiscal year, total net sales were $45.10 billion, compared to $37.59 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $10.05 billion, compared to $8.32 billion. Operating income increased to $10.18 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.82 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.62 billion at the end of the reporting period.
