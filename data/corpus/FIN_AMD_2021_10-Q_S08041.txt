Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $17.92 billion, compared to $14.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $3.62 billion, compared to $2.49 billion. Operating income increased to $4.39 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $1.61 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.79 billion at the end of the reporting period.
