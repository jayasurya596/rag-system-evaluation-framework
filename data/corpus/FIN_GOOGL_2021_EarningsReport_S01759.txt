Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $271.87 billion, compared to $242.74 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $61.74 billion, compared to $47.39 billion. Operating income increased to $71.97 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.44 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $44.97 billion at the end of the reporting period.
