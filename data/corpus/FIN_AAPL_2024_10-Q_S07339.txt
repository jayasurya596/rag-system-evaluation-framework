Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $458.76 billion, compared to $424.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $101.94 billion, compared to $74.32 billion. Operating income increased to $125.31 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.17 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $79.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
