Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $399.68 billion, compared to $370.07 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $82.23 billion, compared to $75.73 billion. Operating income increased to $116.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $139.94 billion at the end of the reporting period.
