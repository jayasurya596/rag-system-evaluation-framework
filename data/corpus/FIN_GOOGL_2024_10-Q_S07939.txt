Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $395.50 billion, compared to $353.13 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $69.03 billion, compared to $64.66 billion. Operating income increased to $111.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.99 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $116.45 billion at the end of the reporting period.
