For the fiscal year, total net sales were $167.11 billion, compared to $104.45 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $30.16 billion, compared to $18.31 billion. Operating income increased to $35.80 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $16.81 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $61.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
