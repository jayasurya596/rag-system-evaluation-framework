For the fiscal year, total net sales were $414.86 billion, compared to $360.75 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $88.51 billion, compared to $85.15 billion. Operating income increased to $85.91 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.02 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $82.92 billion at the end of the reporting period.
