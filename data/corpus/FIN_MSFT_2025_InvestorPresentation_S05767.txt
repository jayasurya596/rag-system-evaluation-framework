For the fiscal year, total net sales were $366.00 billion, compared to $318.26 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $69.71 billion, compared to $78.70 billion. Operating income increased to $85.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.30 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $104.25 billion at the end of the reporting period.
