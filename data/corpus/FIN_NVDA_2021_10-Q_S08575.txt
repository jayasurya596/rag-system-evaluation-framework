Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $39.44 billion, compared to $24.65 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $6.14 billion, compared to $5.31 billion. Operating income increased to $8.56 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.71 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
