Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $403.32 billion, compared to $350.72 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $72.07 billion, compared to $65.25 billion. Operating income increased to $83.68 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.06 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $79.10 billion at the end of the reporting period.
