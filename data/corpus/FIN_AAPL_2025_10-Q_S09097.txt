Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $528.65 billion, compared to $489.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $95.24 billion, compared to $94.08 billion. Operating income increased to $132.80 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.67 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $93.80 billion at the end of the reporting period.
