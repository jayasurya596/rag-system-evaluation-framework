For the fiscal year, total net sales were $478.47 billion, compared to $427.21 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $89.77 billion, compared to $67.98 billion. Operating income increased to $137.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.74 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $61.12 billion at the end of the reporting period.
