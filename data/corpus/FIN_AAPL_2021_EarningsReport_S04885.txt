Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $384.02 billion, compared to $355.58 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $91.65 billion, compared to $88.28 billion. Operating income increased to $94.96 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $41.84 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $136.24 billion at the end of the reporting period.
