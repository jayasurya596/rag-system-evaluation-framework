Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $463.92 billion, compared to $429.55 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $105.90 billion, compared to $81.52 billion. Operating income increased to $123.14 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.35 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $168.57 billion at the end of the reporting period.
