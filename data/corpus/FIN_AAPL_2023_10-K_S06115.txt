Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $432.97 billion, compared to $400.89 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $104.75 billion, compared to $97.39 billion. Operating income increased to $115.00 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.41 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $129.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
