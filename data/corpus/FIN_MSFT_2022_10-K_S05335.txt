Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $248.26 billion, compared to $215.88 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $58.74 billion, compared to $37.74 billion. Operating income increased to $54.64 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $23.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $84.13 billion at the end of the reporting period.
