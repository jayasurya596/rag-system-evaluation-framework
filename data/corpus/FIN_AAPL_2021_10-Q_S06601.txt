Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $379.90 billion, compared to $351.76 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $72.44 billion, compared to $80.44 billion. Operating income increased to $102.58 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $104.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
