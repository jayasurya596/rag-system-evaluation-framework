Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $163.93 billion, compared to $102.45 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $39.45 billion, compared to $18.05 billion. Operating income increased to $36.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $13.45 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.18 billion at the end of the reporting period.
