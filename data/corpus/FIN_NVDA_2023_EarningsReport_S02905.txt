Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $105.46 billion, compared to $65.91 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $17.06 billion, compared to $15.87 billion. Operating income increased to $27.97 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $9.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $19.12 billion at the end of the reporting period.
