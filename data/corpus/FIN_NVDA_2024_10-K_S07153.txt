Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $156.25 billion, compared to $97.66 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $32.01 billion, compared to $14.80 billion. Operating income increased to $45.03 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $12.85 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
