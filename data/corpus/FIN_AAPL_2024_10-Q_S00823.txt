Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $493.14 billion, compared to $456.61 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $106.49 billion, compared to $74.96 billion. Operating income increased to $123.18 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.27 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $158.94 billion at the end of the reporting period.
