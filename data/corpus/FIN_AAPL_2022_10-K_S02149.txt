Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $427.04 billion, compared to $395.41 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $66.83 billion, compared to $69.52 billion. Operating income increased to $101.86 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.59 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $132.84 billion at the end of the reporting period.
