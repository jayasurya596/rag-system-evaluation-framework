Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $294.03 billion, compared to $262.53 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $51.45 billion, compared to $51.15 billion. Operating income increased to $71.17 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.92 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $62.69 billion at the end of the reporting period.
