Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $411.22 billion, compared to $257.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $66.75 billion, compared to $54.70 billion. Operating income increased to $113.53 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.99 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $116.30 billion at the end of the reporting period.
