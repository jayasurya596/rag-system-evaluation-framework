Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $443.18 billion, compared to $395.69 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $104.95 billion, compared to $78.08 billion. Operating income increased to $118.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $39.32 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $72.12 billion at the end of the reporting period.
