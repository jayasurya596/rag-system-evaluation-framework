Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $290.31 billion, compared to $259.21 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $51.41 billion, compared to $50.56 billion. Operating income increased to $65.01 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.25 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $89.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
