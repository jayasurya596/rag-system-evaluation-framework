Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $421.11 billion, compared to $366.19 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $69.74 billion, compared to $90.38 billion. Operating income increased to $98.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.30 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $61.33 billion at the end of the reporting period.
