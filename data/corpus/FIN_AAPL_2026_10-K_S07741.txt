Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $536.34 billion, compared to $496.61 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $110.40 billion, compared to $106.42 billion. Operating income increased to $147.89 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $54.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $135.56 billion at the end of the reporting period.
