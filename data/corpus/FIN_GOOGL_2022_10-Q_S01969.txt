Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $286.02 billion, compared to $255.37 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $65.61 billion, compared to $47.09 billion. Operating income increased to $80.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $92.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
