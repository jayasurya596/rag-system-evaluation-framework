For the fiscal year, total net sales were $350.31 billion, compared to $304.62 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $78.70 billion, compared to $71.66 billion. Operating income increased to $79.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.58 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $90.06 billion at the end of the reporting period.
