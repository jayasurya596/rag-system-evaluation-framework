For the fiscal year, total net sales were $431.19 billion, compared to $269.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $94.43 billion, compared to $48.61 billion. Operating income increased to $89.64 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.48 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $78.73 billion at the end of the reporting period.
