For the fiscal year, total net sales were $44.25 billion, compared to $36.88 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $9.85 billion, compared to $7.41 billion. Operating income increased to $12.40 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.60 billion at the end of the reporting period.
