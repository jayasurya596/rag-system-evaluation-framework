Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $268.98 billion, compared to $240.16 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $64.64 billion, compared to $51.55 billion. Operating income increased to $77.41 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $95.33 billion at the end of the reporting period.
