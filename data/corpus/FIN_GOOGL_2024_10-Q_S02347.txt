Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $395.14 billion, compared to $352.80 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $77.29 billion, compared to $60.63 billion. Operating income increased to $99.88 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.96 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $81.61 billion at the end of the reporting period.
