Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $393.67 billion, compared to $351.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $70.92 billion, compared to $81.97 billion. Operating income increased to $105.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.17 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $134.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
