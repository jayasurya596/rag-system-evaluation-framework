Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $411.97 billion, compared to $358.23 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $79.28 billion, compared to $63.93 billion. Operating income increased to $87.04 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.27 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $153.20 billion at the end of the reporting period.
