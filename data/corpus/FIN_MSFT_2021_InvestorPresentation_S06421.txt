Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $215.25 billion, compared to $187.17 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $36.81 billion, compared to $29.21 billion. Operating income increased to $53.53 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $18.86 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $28.77 billion at the end of the reporting period.
