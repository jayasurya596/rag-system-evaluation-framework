Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $171.46 billion, compared to $107.16 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $39.37 billion, compared to $25.62 billion. Operating income increased to $42.01 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $14.48 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $38.63 billion at the end of the reporting period.
