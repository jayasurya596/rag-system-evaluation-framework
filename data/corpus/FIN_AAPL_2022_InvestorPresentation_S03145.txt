Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $403.33 billion, compared to $373.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $82.70 billion, compared to $66.64 billion. Operating income increased to $93.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $41.42 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $109.50 billion at the end of the reporting period.
