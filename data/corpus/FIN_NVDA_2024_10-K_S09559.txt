Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $160.40 billion, compared to $100.25 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $27.02 billion, compared to $22.51 billion. Operating income increased to $33.22 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $13.42 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $42.90 billion at the end of the reporting period.
