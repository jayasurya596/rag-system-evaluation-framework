For the fiscal year, total net sales were $305.96 billion, compared to $266.05 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $49.90 billion, compared to $50.21 billion. Operating income increased to $81.70 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $99.00 billion at the end of the reporting period.
