For the fiscal year, total net sales were $45.55 billion, compared to $37.96 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.84 billion, compared to $7.39 billion. Operating income increased to $11.15 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.63 billion at the end of the reporting period.
