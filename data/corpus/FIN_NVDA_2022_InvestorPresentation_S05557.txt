Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $61.16 billion, compared to $38.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $13.95 billion, compared to $7.64 billion. Operating income increased to $17.44 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $5.64 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $8.66 billion at the end of the reporting period.
