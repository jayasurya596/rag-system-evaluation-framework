For the fiscal year, total net sales were $249.73 billion, compared to $156.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $59.41 billion, compared to $37.35 billion. Operating income increased to $59.33 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.83 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $72.31 billion at the end of the reporting period.
