Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $388.43 billion, compared to $359.65 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $86.26 billion, compared to $69.56 billion. Operating income increased to $115.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.08 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $118.04 billion at the end of the reporting period.
