Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $468.64 billion, compared to $433.93 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $81.00 billion, compared to $89.99 billion. Operating income increased to $125.56 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.33 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $79.77 billion at the end of the reporting period.
