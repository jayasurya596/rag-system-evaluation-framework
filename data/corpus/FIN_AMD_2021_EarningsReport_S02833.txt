Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $17.97 billion, compared to $14.98 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $3.76 billion, compared to $2.53 billion. Operating income increased to $4.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $1.96 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.07 billion at the end of the reporting period.
