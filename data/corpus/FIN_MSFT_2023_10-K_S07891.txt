Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $266.81 billion, compared to $232.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $54.04 billion, compared to $57.38 billion. Operating income increased to $78.92 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.29 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $31.99 billion at the end of the reporting period.
