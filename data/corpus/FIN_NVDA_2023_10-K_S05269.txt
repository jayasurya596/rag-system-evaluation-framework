Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $102.42 billion, compared to $64.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $18.44 billion, compared to $10.35 billion. Operating income increased to $28.28 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $11.30 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $19.76 billion at the end of the reporting period.
