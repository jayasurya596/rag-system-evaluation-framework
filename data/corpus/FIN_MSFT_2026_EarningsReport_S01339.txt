For the fiscal year, total net sales were $416.95 billion, compared to $362.56 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $70.24 billion, compared to $77.50 billion. Operating income increased to $99.83 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $146.50 billion at the end of the reporting period.
