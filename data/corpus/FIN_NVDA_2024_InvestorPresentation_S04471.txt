For the fiscal year, total net sales were $162.80 billion, compared to $101.75 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $34.61 billion, compared to $24.64 billion. Operating income increased to $32.58 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $18.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $24.79 billion at the end of the reporting period.
