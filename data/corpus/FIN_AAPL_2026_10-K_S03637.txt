Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $566.00 billion, compared to $524.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $108.53 billion, compared to $92.61 billion. Operating income increased to $139.25 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $59.49 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $171.19 billion at the end of the reporting period.
