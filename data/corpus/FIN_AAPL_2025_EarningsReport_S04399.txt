Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $530.65 billion, compared to $491.35 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $109.68 billion, compared to $105.91 billion. Operating income increased to $128.98 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.89 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $71.43 billion at the end of the reporting period.
