Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $264.08 billion, compared to $229.63 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $57.64 billion, compared to $49.06 billion. Operating income increased to $57.17 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $23.82 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $64.34 billion at the end of the reporting period.
