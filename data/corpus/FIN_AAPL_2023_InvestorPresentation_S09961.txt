Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $440.62 billion, compared to $407.98 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $67.50 billion, compared to $88.44 billion. Operating income increased to $123.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.96 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $99.30 billion at the end of the reporting period.
