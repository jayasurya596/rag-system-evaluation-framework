Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $107.30 billion, compared to $67.06 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $23.73 billion, compared to $11.11 billion. Operating income increased to $29.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $9.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $36.96 billion at the end of the reporting period.
