Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $320.08 billion, compared to $278.33 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $49.46 billion, compared to $54.03 billion. Operating income increased to $78.94 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $86.29 billion at the end of the reporting period.
