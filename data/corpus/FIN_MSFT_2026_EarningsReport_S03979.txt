For the fiscal year, total net sales were $427.04 billion, compared to $371.34 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $93.61 billion, compared to $69.07 billion. Operating income increased to $118.79 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.62 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $169.23 billion at the end of the reporting period.
