For the fiscal year, total net sales were $455.96 billion, compared to $407.11 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $87.16 billion, compared to $73.53 billion. Operating income increased to $126.88 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.56 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $139.45 billion at the end of the reporting period.
