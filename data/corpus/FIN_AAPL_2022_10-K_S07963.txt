Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $387.91 billion, compared to $359.17 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $89.54 billion, compared to $64.07 billion. Operating income increased to $88.80 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.61 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $74.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
