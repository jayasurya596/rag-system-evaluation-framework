For the fiscal year, total net sales were $426.98 billion, compared to $381.23 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $89.42 billion, compared to $87.43 billion. Operating income increased to $87.62 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.28 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $162.20 billion at the end of the reporting period.
