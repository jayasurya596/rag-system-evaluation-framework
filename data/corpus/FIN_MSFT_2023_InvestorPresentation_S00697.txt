Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $279.35 billion, compared to $242.91 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $52.43 billion, compared to $60.27 billion. Operating income increased to $65.18 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.60 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $45.58 billion at the end of the reporting period.
