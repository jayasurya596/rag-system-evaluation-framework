Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $348.33 billion, compared to $311.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $70.82 billion, compared to $59.21 billion. Operating income increased to $95.95 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.12 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $40.24 billion at the end of the reporting period.
