Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $546.85 billion, compared to $506.35 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $95.17 billion, compared to $108.47 billion. Operating income increased to $159.60 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.08 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $61.23 billion at the end of the reporting period.
