Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $479.67 billion, compared to $428.28 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $79.04 billion, compared to $102.03 billion. Operating income increased to $97.05 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.30 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $191.85 billion at the end of the reporting period.
