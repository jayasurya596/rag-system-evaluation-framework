For the fiscal year, total net sales were $560.57 billion, compared to $519.05 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $101.89 billion, compared to $126.92 billion. Operating income increased to $157.66 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $53.98 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $72.73 billion at the end of the reporting period.
