Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $368.80 billion, compared to $329.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $62.57 billion, compared to $70.53 billion. Operating income increased to $89.43 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.66 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $98.83 billion at the end of the reporting period.
