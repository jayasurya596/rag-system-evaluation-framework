Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $257.01 billion, compared to $229.47 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $41.22 billion, compared to $51.90 billion. Operating income increased to $60.33 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $31.49 billion at the end of the reporting period.
