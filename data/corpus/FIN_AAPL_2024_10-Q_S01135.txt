Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $465.44 billion, compared to $430.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $96.12 billion, compared to $99.18 billion. Operating income increased to $118.02 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $81.90 billion at the end of the reporting period.
