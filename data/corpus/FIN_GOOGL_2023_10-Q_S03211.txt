Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $326.46 billion, compared to $291.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $52.54 billion, compared to $62.73 billion. Operating income increased to $66.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.32 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $111.61 billion at the end of the reporting period.
