Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $420.07 billion, compared to $388.95 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $85.09 billion, compared to $63.00 billion. Operating income increased to $107.06 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.53 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $165.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
