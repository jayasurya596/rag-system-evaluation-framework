For the fiscal year, total net sales were $169.16 billion, compared to $105.72 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $34.03 billion, compared to $21.93 billion. Operating income increased to $36.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $19.23 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $43.54 billion at the end of the reporting period.
