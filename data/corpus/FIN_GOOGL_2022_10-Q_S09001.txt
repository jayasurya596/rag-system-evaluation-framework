Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $293.33 billion, compared to $261.90 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $63.35 billion, compared to $53.45 billion. Operating income increased to $83.79 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $24.14 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $111.39 billion at the end of the reporting period.
