Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $243.92 billion, compared to $212.11 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $47.17 billion, compared to $33.90 billion. Operating income increased to $64.24 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.03 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $56.86 billion at the end of the reporting period.
