Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $419.32 billion, compared to $388.26 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $95.55 billion, compared to $78.08 billion. Operating income increased to $120.52 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.91 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $69.44 billion at the end of the reporting period.
