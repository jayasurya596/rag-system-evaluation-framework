Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $357.59 billion, compared to $310.95 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $77.26 billion, compared to $72.13 billion. Operating income increased to $76.60 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $131.21 billion at the end of the reporting period.
