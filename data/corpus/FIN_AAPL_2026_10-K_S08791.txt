Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $558.60 billion, compared to $517.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $89.43 billion, compared to $81.89 billion. Operating income increased to $160.17 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $66.60 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $221.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
