Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $106.53 billion, compared to $66.58 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $20.38 billion, compared to $10.55 billion. Operating income increased to $22.92 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $12.09 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $37.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
