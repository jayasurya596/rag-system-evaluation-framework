Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $478.79 billion, compared to $443.32 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $78.63 billion, compared to $89.07 billion. Operating income increased to $97.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.15 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $76.60 billion at the end of the reporting period.
