Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $277.29 billion, compared to $241.12 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $57.98 billion, compared to $56.96 billion. Operating income increased to $56.14 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.54 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $59.88 billion at the end of the reporting period.
