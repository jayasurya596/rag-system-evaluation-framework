Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $99.10 billion, compared to $61.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $22.85 billion, compared to $12.88 billion. Operating income increased to $20.52 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $8.71 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $20.57 billion at the end of the reporting period.
