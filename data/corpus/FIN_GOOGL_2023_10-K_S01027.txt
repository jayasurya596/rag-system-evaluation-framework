Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $335.77 billion, compared to $299.80 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $83.64 billion, compared to $52.86 billion. Operating income increased to $93.80 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.67 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $111.22 billion at the end of the reporting period.
