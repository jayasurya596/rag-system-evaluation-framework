Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $446.80 billion, compared to $413.71 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $109.77 billion, compared to $67.42 billion. Operating income increased to $122.59 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.54 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $95.66 billion at the end of the reporting period.
