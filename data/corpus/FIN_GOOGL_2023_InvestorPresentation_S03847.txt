Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $343.24 billion, compared to $306.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $57.04 billion, compared to $66.50 billion. Operating income increased to $98.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.22 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $120.89 billion at the end of the reporting period.
