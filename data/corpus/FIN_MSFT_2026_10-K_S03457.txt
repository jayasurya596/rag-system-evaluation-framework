Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $435.24 billion, compared to $378.47 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $104.05 billion, compared to $60.74 billion. Operating income increased to $93.99 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $41.55 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $124.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
