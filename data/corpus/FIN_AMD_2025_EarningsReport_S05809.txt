For the fiscal year, total net sales were $37.23 billion, compared to $31.03 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.79 billion, compared to $5.03 billion. Operating income increased to $10.10 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.17 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
