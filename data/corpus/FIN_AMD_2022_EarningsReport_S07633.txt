========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $21.73 billion, compared to $18.11 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $4.27 billion, compared to $4.32 billion. Operating income increased to $4.84 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $1.76 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.08 billion at the end of the reporting period.
