Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $206.26 billion, compared to $179.36 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $49.18 billion, compared to $35.31 billion. Operating income increased to $51.36 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $24.39 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $69.42 billion at the end of the reporting period.
