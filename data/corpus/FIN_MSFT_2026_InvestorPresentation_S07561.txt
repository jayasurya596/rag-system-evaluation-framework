For the fiscal year, total net sales were $397.92 billion, compared to $346.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $64.57 billion, compared to $62.91 billion. Operating income increased to $98.36 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.31 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $85.46 billion at the end of the reporting period.
