Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $61.25 billion, compared to $38.28 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $10.43 billion, compared to $7.76 billion. Operating income increased to $18.05 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $5.71 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
