For the fiscal year, total net sales were $344.04 billion, compared to $299.16 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $58.89 billion, compared to $72.23 billion. Operating income increased to $88.73 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.51 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $96.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
