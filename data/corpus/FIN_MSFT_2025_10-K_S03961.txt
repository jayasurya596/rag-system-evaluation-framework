Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $365.80 billion, compared to $318.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $84.14 billion, compared to $72.23 billion. Operating income increased to $86.68 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $43.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
