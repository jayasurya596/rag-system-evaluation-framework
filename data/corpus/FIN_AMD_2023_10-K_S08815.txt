For the fiscal year, total net sales were $25.93 billion, compared to $21.61 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $4.02 billion, compared to $4.90 billion. Operating income increased to $7.72 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.00 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $8.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
