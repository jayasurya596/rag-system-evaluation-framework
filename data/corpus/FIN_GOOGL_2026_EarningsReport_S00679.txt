For the fiscal year, total net sales were $469.53 billion, compared to $419.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $111.58 billion, compared to $93.39 billion. Operating income increased to $98.08 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.51 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $156.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
