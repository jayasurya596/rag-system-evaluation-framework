Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $38.85 billion, compared to $32.38 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.52 billion, compared to $7.64 billion. Operating income increased to $10.36 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.39 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.71 billion at the end of the reporting period.
