Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $476.51 billion, compared to $441.21 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $83.61 billion, compared to $85.73 billion. Operating income increased to $116.34 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.00 billion at the end of the reporting period.
