For the fiscal year, total net sales were $402.76 billion, compared to $251.72 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $90.07 billion, compared to $42.97 billion. Operating income increased to $117.34 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.25 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $153.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
