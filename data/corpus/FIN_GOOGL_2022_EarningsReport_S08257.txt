Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $294.38 billion, compared to $262.84 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $60.62 billion, compared to $40.23 billion. Operating income increased to $80.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $30.43 billion at the end of the reporting period.
