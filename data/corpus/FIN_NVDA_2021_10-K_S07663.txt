Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $38.13 billion, compared to $23.83 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $9.39 billion, compared to $5.61 billion. Operating income increased to $8.00 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.97 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.40 billion at the end of the reporting period.
