Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $38.41 billion, compared to $24.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $7.18 billion, compared to $5.26 billion. Operating income increased to $7.75 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.69 billion at the end of the reporting period.
