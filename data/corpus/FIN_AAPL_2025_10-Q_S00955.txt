Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $518.63 billion, compared to $480.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $110.17 billion, compared to $99.16 billion. Operating income increased to $125.27 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $57.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $168.77 billion at the end of the reporting period.
