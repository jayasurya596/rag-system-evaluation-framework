Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $434.91 billion, compared to $402.70 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $89.27 billion, compared to $92.68 billion. Operating income increased to $110.63 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.76 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $168.49 billion at the end of the reporting period.
