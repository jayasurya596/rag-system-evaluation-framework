Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $548.10 billion, compared to $507.50 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $114.81 billion, compared to $99.59 billion. Operating income increased to $140.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $59.50 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $85.19 billion at the end of the reporting period.
