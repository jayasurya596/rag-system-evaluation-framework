Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $42.98 billion, compared to $35.82 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $10.50 billion, compared to $8.42 billion. Operating income increased to $11.75 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $5.12 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $16.62 billion at the end of the reporting period.
