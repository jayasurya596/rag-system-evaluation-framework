Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $318.47 billion, compared to $276.93 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $49.16 billion, compared to $59.99 billion. Operating income increased to $66.84 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.08 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $56.67 billion at the end of the reporting period.
