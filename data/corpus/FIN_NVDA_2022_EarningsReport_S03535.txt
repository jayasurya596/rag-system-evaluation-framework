Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $66.84 billion, compared to $41.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $10.49 billion, compared to $6.95 billion. Operating income increased to $18.66 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $6.08 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $23.73 billion at the end of the reporting period.
