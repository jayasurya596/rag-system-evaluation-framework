For the fiscal year, total net sales were $421.42 billion, compared to $376.27 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $87.34 billion, compared to $77.82 billion. Operating income increased to $106.15 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.45 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $90.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
