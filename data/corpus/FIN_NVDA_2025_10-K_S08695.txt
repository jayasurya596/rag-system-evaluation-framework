Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $272.97 billion, compared to $170.61 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $66.92 billion, compared to $39.89 billion. Operating income increased to $79.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.45 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $27.64 billion at the end of the reporting period.
