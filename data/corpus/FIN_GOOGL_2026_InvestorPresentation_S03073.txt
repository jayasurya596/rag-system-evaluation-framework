For the fiscal year, total net sales were $495.14 billion, compared to $442.09 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $78.01 billion, compared to $92.18 billion. Operating income increased to $122.53 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.93 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $136.85 billion at the end of the reporting period.
