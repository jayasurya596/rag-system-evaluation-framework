Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $166.04 billion, compared to $103.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $29.82 billion, compared to $17.78 billion. Operating income increased to $39.82 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $16.61 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $33.84 billion at the end of the reporting period.
