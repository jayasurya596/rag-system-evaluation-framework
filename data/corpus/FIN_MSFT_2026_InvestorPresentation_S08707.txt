For the fiscal year, total net sales were $423.65 billion, compared to $368.39 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $85.55 billion, compared to $79.88 billion. Operating income increased to $104.23 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $48.14 billion at the end of the reporting period.
