Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $538.27 billion, compared to $498.40 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $115.90 billion, compared to $83.13 billion. Operating income increased to $118.51 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $164.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
