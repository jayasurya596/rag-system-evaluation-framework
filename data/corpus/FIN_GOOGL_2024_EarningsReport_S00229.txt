Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $393.51 billion, compared to $351.35 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $66.07 billion, compared to $86.44 billion. Operating income increased to $80.09 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.12 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $123.64 billion at the end of the reporting period.
