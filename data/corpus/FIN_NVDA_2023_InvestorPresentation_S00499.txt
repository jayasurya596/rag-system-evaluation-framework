Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $107.21 billion, compared to $67.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $24.17 billion, compared to $16.71 billion. Operating income increased to $28.59 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $12.01 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $34.15 billion at the end of the reporting period.
