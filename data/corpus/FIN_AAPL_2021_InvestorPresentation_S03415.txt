Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $391.56 billion, compared to $362.55 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $60.71 billion, compared to $68.82 billion. Operating income increased to $93.87 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $90.07 billion at the end of the reporting period.
