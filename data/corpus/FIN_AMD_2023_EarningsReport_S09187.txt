Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $25.87 billion, compared to $21.56 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.18 billion, compared to $3.76 billion. Operating income increased to $7.04 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.55 billion at the end of the reporting period.
