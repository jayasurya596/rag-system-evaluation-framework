Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $266.01 billion, compared to $237.51 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $51.89 billion, compared to $43.30 billion. Operating income increased to $63.91 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $63.66 billion at the end of the reporting period.
