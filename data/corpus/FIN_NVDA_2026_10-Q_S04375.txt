For the fiscal year, total net sales were $433.39 billion, compared to $270.87 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $84.23 billion, compared to $44.01 billion. Operating income increased to $113.23 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $39.62 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $163.74 billion at the end of the reporting period.
