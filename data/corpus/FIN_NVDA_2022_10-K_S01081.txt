Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $66.38 billion, compared to $41.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $16.11 billion, compared to $6.27 billion. Operating income increased to $17.08 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $6.11 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.61 billion at the end of the reporting period.
