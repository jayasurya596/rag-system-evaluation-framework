Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $310.92 billion, compared to $270.36 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $73.36 billion, compared to $58.20 billion. Operating income increased to $70.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $116.76 billion at the end of the reporting period.
