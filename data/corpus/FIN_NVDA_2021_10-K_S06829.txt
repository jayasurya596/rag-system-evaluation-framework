Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $41.52 billion, compared to $25.95 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $7.53 billion, compared to $4.47 billion. Operating income increased to $10.01 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $13.56 billion at the end of the reporting period.
