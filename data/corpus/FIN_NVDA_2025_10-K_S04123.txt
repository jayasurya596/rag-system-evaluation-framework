Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $271.39 billion, compared to $169.62 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $58.18 billion, compared to $37.25 billion. Operating income increased to $65.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.97 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $99.71 billion at the end of the reporting period.
