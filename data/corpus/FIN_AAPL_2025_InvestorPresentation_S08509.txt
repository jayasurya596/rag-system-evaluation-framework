Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $511.27 billion, compared to $473.40 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $111.34 billion, compared to $117.61 billion. Operating income increased to $118.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $52.50 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $95.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
