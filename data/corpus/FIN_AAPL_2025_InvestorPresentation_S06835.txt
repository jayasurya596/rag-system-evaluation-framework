Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $534.43 billion, compared to $494.84 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $89.62 billion, compared to $85.08 billion. Operating income increased to $128.35 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $60.30 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $199.77 billion at the end of the reporting period.
