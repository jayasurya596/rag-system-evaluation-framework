Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $538.20 billion, compared to $498.34 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $94.24 billion, compared to $117.05 billion. Operating income increased to $118.29 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.87 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $156.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
