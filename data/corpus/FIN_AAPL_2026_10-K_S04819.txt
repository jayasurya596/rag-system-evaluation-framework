Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $579.76 billion, compared to $536.82 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $134.12 billion, compared to $120.16 billion. Operating income increased to $151.70 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $52.91 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $182.86 billion at the end of the reporting period.
