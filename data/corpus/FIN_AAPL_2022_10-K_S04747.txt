Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $412.33 billion, compared to $381.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $72.21 billion, compared to $60.43 billion. Operating income increased to $89.04 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.10 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $107.55 billion at the end of the reporting period.
