Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $395.37 billion, compared to $353.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $77.97 billion, compared to $75.83 billion. Operating income increased to $93.70 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.38 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $125.71 billion at the end of the reporting period.
