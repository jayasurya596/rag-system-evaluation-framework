For the fiscal year, total net sales were $274.53 billion, compared to $171.58 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $67.10 billion, compared to $37.05 billion. Operating income increased to $75.73 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.81 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $44.82 billion at the end of the reporting period.
