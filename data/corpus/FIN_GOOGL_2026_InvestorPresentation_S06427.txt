For the fiscal year, total net sales were $477.78 billion, compared to $426.59 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $114.74 billion, compared to $73.31 billion. Operating income increased to $111.22 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $55.80 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $138.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
