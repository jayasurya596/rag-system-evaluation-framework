Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $521.12 billion, compared to $482.52 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $126.66 billion, compared to $111.13 billion. Operating income increased to $121.31 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $61.48 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $149.64 billion at the end of the reporting period.
