Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $20.97 billion, compared to $17.47 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $4.63 billion, compared to $2.90 billion. Operating income increased to $4.79 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.01 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $3.80 billion at the end of the reporting period.
