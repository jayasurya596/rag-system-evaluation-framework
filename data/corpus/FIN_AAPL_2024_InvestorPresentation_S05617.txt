Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $486.24 billion, compared to $450.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $83.86 billion, compared to $73.52 billion. Operating income increased to $130.41 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $167.88 billion at the end of the reporting period.
