Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $207.68 billion, compared to $180.59 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $32.56 billion, compared to $38.08 billion. Operating income increased to $48.99 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.40 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $37.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
