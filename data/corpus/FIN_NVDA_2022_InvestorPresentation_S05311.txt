Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $62.17 billion, compared to $38.86 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $15.43 billion, compared to $6.38 billion. Operating income increased to $13.25 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $7.02 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $23.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
