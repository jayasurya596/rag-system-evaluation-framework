Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $393.90 billion, compared to $364.72 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $81.17 billion, compared to $64.99 billion. Operating income increased to $101.56 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.65 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $151.87 billion at the end of the reporting period.
