Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $64.67 billion, compared to $40.42 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $12.24 billion, compared to $9.72 billion. Operating income increased to $17.40 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $6.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $15.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
