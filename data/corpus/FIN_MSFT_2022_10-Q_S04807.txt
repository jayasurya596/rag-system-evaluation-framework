Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $232.45 billion, compared to $202.13 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $39.01 billion, compared to $48.01 billion. Operating income increased to $52.41 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.56 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $25.15 billion at the end of the reporting period.
