Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $165.24 billion, compared to $103.28 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $33.14 billion, compared to $21.62 billion. Operating income increased to $34.75 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $16.76 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $63.02 billion at the end of the reporting period.
