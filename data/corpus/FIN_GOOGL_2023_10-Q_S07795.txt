Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $351.85 billion, compared to $314.16 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $83.06 billion, compared to $67.34 billion. Operating income increased to $98.35 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.00 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $87.76 billion at the end of the reporting period.
