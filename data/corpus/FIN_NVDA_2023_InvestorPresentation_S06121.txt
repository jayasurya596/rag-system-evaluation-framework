Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $107.39 billion, compared to $67.12 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $21.22 billion, compared to $10.52 billion. Operating income increased to $28.24 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $8.78 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $42.22 billion at the end of the reporting period.
