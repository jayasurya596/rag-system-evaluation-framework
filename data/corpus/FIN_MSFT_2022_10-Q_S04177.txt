Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $247.09 billion, compared to $214.86 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $37.91 billion, compared to $43.07 billion. Operating income increased to $69.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.74 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $54.98 billion at the end of the reporting period.
