Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $391.15 billion, compared to $362.17 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $82.86 billion, compared to $77.31 billion. Operating income increased to $84.59 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.35 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $120.58 billion at the end of the reporting period.
