Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $66.52 billion, compared to $41.57 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $10.51 billion, compared to $6.60 billion. Operating income increased to $19.32 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $7.61 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $21.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
