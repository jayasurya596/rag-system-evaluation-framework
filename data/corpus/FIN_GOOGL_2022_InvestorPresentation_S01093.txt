Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $290.28 billion, compared to $259.18 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $65.33 billion, compared to $49.75 billion. Operating income increased to $58.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $24.84 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $56.61 billion at the end of the reporting period.
