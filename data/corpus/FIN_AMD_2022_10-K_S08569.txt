Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $21.60 billion, compared to $18.00 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $3.44 billion, compared to $3.35 billion. Operating income increased to $5.47 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.09 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.51 billion at the end of the reporting period.
