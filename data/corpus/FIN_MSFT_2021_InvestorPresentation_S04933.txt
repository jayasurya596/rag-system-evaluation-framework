Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $214.26 billion, compared to $186.31 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $36.86 billion, compared to $32.88 billion. Operating income increased to $49.23 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $17.63 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $64.33 billion at the end of the reporting period.
