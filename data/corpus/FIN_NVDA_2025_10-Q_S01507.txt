Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $273.93 billion, compared to $171.21 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $62.24 billion, compared to $36.86 billion. Operating income increased to $63.66 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.06 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $81.63 billion at the end of the reporting period.
