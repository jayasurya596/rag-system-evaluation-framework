Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $433.09 billion, compared to $401.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $78.76 billion, compared to $67.31 billion. Operating income increased to $92.82 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.75 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $138.76 billion at the end of the reporting period.
