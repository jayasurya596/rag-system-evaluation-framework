Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $528.93 billion, compared to $489.75 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $103.06 billion, compared to $91.46 billion. Operating income increased to $148.47 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $51.54 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $147.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
