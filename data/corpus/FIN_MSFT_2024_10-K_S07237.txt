Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $318.38 billion, compared to $276.85 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $60.11 billion, compared to $49.65 billion. Operating income increased to $70.27 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $62.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
