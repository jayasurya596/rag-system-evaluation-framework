Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $41.56 billion, compared to $25.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $9.68 billion, compared to $4.89 billion. Operating income increased to $11.11 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.70 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.24 billion at the end of the reporting period.
