Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $453.95 billion, compared to $420.33 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $84.66 billion, compared to $68.57 billion. Operating income increased to $132.08 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.25 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $101.98 billion at the end of the reporting period.
