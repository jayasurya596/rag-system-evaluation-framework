For the fiscal year, total net sales were $362.48 billion, compared to $323.64 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $67.52 billion, compared to $51.63 billion. Operating income increased to $73.56 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.46 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $129.73 billion at the end of the reporting period.
