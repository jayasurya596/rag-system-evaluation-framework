Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $308.61 billion, compared to $268.36 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $64.69 billion, compared to $44.88 billion. Operating income increased to $84.72 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.27 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $122.63 billion at the end of the reporting period.
