Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $265.24 billion, compared to $236.83 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $63.34 billion, compared to $40.30 billion. Operating income increased to $70.82 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.41 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $49.04 billion at the end of the reporting period.
