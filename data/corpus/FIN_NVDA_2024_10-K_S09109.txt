Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $164.39 billion, compared to $102.74 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $29.05 billion, compared to $21.66 billion. Operating income increased to $40.96 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $16.19 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $55.21 billion at the end of the reporting period.
