Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $363.52 billion, compared to $336.59 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $68.14 billion, compared to $66.21 billion. Operating income increased to $106.40 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $133.94 billion at the end of the reporting period.
