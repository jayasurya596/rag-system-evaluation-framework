Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $364.04 billion, compared to $325.03 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $82.89 billion, compared to $53.17 billion. Operating income increased to $74.27 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $39.55 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $139.40 billion at the end of the reporting period.
