Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $164.06 billion, compared to $102.54 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $27.69 billion, compared to $16.86 billion. Operating income increased to $33.81 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $14.10 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $32.46 billion at the end of the reporting period.
