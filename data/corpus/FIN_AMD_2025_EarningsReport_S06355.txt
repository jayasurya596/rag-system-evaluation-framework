For the fiscal year, total net sales were $36.57 billion, compared to $30.48 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $8.30 billion, compared to $5.45 billion. Operating income increased to $8.41 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.56 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $11.07 billion at the end of the reporting period.
