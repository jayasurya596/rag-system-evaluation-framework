Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $303.58 billion, compared to $271.05 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $49.65 billion, compared to $60.18 billion. Operating income increased to $83.94 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $115.69 billion at the end of the reporting period.
