Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $308.87 billion, compared to $275.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $64.04 billion, compared to $51.82 billion. Operating income increased to $85.60 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.46 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $67.85 billion at the end of the reporting period.
