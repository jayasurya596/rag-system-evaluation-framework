Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $313.60 billion, compared to $272.69 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $78.20 billion, compared to $53.28 billion. Operating income increased to $68.68 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.78 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $88.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
