For the fiscal year, total net sales were $249.10 billion, compared to $216.61 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $53.72 billion, compared to $42.94 billion. Operating income increased to $65.23 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.40 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $32.15 billion at the end of the reporting period.
