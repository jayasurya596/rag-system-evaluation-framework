For the fiscal year, total net sales were $260.29 billion, compared to $226.34 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $64.16 billion, compared to $37.15 billion. Operating income increased to $65.91 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $39.23 billion at the end of the reporting period.
