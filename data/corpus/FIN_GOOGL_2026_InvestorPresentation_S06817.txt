For the fiscal year, total net sales were $496.78 billion, compared to $443.56 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $110.30 billion, compared to $89.98 billion. Research and Development expenses were $49.60 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure.
