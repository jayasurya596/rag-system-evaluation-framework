Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $101.14 billion, compared to $63.21 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $25.05 billion, compared to $11.93 billion. Operating income increased to $25.39 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $10.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
