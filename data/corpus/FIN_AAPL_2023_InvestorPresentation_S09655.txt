Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $457.12 billion, compared to $423.26 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $98.74 billion, compared to $95.17 billion. Operating income increased to $133.17 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $51.95 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $177.88 billion at the end of the reporting period.
