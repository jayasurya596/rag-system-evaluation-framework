Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $515.14 billion, compared to $476.98 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $88.36 billion, compared to $99.78 billion. Operating income increased to $133.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.80 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $179.60 billion at the end of the reporting period.
