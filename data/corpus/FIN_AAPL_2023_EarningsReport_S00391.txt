Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $457.64 billion, compared to $423.74 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $88.52 billion, compared to $102.98 billion. Operating income increased to $97.05 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.71 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $148.42 billion at the end of the reporting period.
