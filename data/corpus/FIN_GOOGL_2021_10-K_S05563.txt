Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $272.43 billion, compared to $243.24 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $44.65 billion, compared to $46.79 billion. Operating income increased to $75.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.13 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $95.62 billion at the end of the reporting period.
