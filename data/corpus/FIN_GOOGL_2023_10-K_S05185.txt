Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $349.25 billion, compared to $311.83 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $86.85 billion, compared to $74.15 billion. Operating income increased to $96.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $40.04 billion at the end of the reporting period.
