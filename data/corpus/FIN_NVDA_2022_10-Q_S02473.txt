Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $63.54 billion, compared to $39.71 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $12.98 billion, compared to $9.57 billion. Operating income increased to $16.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $6.33 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $13.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
