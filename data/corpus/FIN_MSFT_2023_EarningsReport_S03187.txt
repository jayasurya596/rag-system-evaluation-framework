Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $276.69 billion, compared to $240.60 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $46.87 billion, compared to $37.84 billion. Operating income increased to $66.88 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.97 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $101.12 billion at the end of the reporting period.
