Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $99.86 billion, compared to $62.41 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $18.19 billion, compared to $13.07 billion. Operating income increased to $25.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $11.75 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $24.52 billion at the end of the reporting period.
