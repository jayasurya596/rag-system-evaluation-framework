Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $291.64 billion, compared to $260.39 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $68.30 billion, compared to $55.19 billion. Operating income increased to $83.57 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.43 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $71.56 billion at the end of the reporting period.
