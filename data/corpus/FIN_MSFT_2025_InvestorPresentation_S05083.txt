Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $375.67 billion, compared to $326.67 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $56.98 billion, compared to $61.69 billion. Operating income increased to $86.73 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $39.31 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $83.66 billion at the end of the reporting period.
