Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $236.31 billion, compared to $205.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $58.57 billion, compared to $37.62 billion. Operating income increased to $64.09 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $23.56 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $65.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
