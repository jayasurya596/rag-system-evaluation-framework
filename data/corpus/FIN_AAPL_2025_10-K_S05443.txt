Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $530.07 billion, compared to $490.81 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $87.35 billion, compared to $107.09 billion. Operating income increased to $120.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $58.83 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $67.53 billion at the end of the reporting period.
