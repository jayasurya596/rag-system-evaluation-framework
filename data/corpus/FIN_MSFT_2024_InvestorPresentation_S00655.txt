Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $330.32 billion, compared to $287.24 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $67.81 billion, compared to $71.67 billion. Operating income increased to $68.59 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.82 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $108.99 billion at the end of the reporting period.
