For the fiscal year, total net sales were $300.04 billion, compared to $267.89 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $50.19 billion, compared to $62.62 billion. Operating income increased to $82.94 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.70 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $75.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
