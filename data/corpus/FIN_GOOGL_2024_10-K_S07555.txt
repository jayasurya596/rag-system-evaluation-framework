Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $368.84 billion, compared to $329.32 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $83.48 billion, compared to $78.73 billion. Operating income increased to $75.96 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.56 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $121.82 billion at the end of the reporting period.
