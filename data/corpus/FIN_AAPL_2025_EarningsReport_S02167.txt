Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $518.73 billion, compared to $480.31 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $129.18 billion, compared to $87.54 billion. Operating income increased to $116.50 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.99 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $163.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
