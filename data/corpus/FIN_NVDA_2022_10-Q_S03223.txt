Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $62.26 billion, compared to $38.92 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $14.59 billion, compared to $8.34 billion. Operating income increased to $18.02 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $7.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $8.59 billion at the end of the reporting period.
