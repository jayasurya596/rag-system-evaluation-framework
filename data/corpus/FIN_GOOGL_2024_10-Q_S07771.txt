Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $369.85 billion, compared to $330.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $75.31 billion, compared to $70.53 billion. Operating income increased to $91.71 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.47 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $37.59 billion at the end of the reporting period.
