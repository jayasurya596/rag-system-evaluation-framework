For the fiscal year, total net sales were $424.45 billion, compared to $265.28 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $75.08 billion, compared to $46.41 billion. Operating income increased to $100.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.93 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $153.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
