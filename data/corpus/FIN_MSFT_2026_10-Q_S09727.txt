For the fiscal year, total net sales were $402.26 billion, compared to $349.79 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $77.29 billion, compared to $81.60 billion. Operating income increased to $110.11 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.01 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $146.86 billion at the end of the reporting period.
