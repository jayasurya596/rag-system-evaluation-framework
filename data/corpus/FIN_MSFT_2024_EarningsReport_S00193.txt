For the fiscal year, total net sales were $307.90 billion, compared to $267.74 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $70.38 billion, compared to $49.93 billion. Operating income increased to $90.28 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.09 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $122.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
