Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $31.69 billion, compared to $26.41 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.56 billion, compared to $4.66 billion. Operating income increased to $8.94 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.20 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $7.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
