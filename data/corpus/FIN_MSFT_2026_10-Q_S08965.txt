For the fiscal year, total net sales were $417.90 billion, compared to $363.39 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $103.56 billion, compared to $59.27 billion. Operating income increased to $94.87 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $39.56 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $105.41 billion at the end of the reporting period.
