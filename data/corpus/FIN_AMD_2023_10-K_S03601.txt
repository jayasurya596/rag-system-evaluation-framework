Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $26.49 billion, compared to $22.07 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $5.90 billion, compared to $4.19 billion. Operating income increased to $7.05 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.96 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.18 billion at the end of the reporting period.
