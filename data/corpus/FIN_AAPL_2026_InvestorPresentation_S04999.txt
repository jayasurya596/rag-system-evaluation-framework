For the fiscal year, total net sales were $566.23 billion, compared to $524.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $102.43 billion, compared to $102.48 billion. Operating income increased to $160.29 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $51.24 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $157.88 billion at the end of the reporting period.
