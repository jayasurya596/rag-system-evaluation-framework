Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $414.56 billion, compared to $360.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $96.26 billion, compared to $81.33 billion. Operating income increased to $97.23 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.82 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $152.31 billion at the end of the reporting period.
