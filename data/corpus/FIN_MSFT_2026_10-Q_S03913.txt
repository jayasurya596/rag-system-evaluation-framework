For the fiscal year, total net sales were $396.49 billion, compared to $344.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $66.64 billion, compared to $63.75 billion. Operating income increased to $97.81 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.53 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $139.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
