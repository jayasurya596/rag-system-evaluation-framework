For the fiscal year, total net sales were $168.36 billion, compared to $105.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $30.99 billion, compared to $22.70 billion. Operating income increased to $48.08 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $19.47 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $17.33 billion at the end of the reporting period.
