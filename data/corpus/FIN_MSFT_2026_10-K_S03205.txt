Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $432.12 billion, compared to $375.76 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $99.11 billion, compared to $85.14 billion. Operating income increased to $129.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.78 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $43.52 billion at the end of the reporting period.
