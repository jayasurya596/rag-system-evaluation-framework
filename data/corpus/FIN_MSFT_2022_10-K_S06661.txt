Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $237.70 billion, compared to $206.70 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $45.97 billion, compared to $33.27 billion. Operating income increased to $50.49 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $24.87 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $44.82 billion at the end of the reporting period.
