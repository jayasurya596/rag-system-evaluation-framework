Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $300.15 billion, compared to $267.99 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $53.05 billion, compared to $61.83 billion. Operating income increased to $63.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.47 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $58.53 billion at the end of the reporting period.
