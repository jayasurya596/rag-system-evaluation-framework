Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $279.86 billion, compared to $243.35 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $68.33 billion, compared to $50.93 billion. Operating income increased to $82.62 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.99 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $93.58 billion at the end of the reporting period.
