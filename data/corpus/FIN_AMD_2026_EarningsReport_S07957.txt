For the fiscal year, total net sales were $43.81 billion, compared to $36.50 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.57 billion, compared to $8.34 billion. Operating income increased to $9.00 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.59 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $12.60 billion at the end of the reporting period.
