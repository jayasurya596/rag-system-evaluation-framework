Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $416.57 billion, compared to $371.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $86.32 billion, compared to $66.28 billion. Operating income increased to $94.61 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.98 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $47.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
