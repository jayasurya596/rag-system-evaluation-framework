For the fiscal year, total net sales were $483.87 billion, compared to $432.03 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $88.30 billion, compared to $97.83 billion. Operating income increased to $139.44 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.42 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $182.45 billion at the end of the reporting period.
