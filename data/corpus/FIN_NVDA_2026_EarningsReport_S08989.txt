For the fiscal year, total net sales were $431.64 billion, compared to $269.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $68.94 billion, compared to $59.88 billion. Operating income increased to $94.36 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $41.06 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $45.23 billion at the end of the reporting period.
