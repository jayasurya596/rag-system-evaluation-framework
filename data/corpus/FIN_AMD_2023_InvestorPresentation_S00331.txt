Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $26.74 billion, compared to $22.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $4.93 billion, compared to $5.11 billion. Operating income increased to $7.72 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.15 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.75 billion at the end of the reporting period.
