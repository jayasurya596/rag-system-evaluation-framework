Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $345.58 billion, compared to $300.51 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $60.42 billion, compared to $70.81 billion. Operating income increased to $81.23 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.43 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $123.20 billion at the end of the reporting period.
