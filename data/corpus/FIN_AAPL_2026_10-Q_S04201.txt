Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $542.60 billion, compared to $502.40 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $87.63 billion, compared to $80.50 billion. Operating income increased to $109.42 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.92 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $116.18 billion at the end of the reporting period.
