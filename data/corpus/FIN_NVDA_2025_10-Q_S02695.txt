Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $255.20 billion, compared to $159.50 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $48.15 billion, compared to $33.16 billion. Operating income increased to $63.03 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.68 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.37 billion at the end of the reporting period.
