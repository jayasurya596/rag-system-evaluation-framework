Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $230.28 billion, compared to $200.24 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $38.06 billion, compared to $45.24 billion. Operating income increased to $47.79 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.41 billion at the end of the reporting period.
