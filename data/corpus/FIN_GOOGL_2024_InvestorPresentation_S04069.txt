For the fiscal year, total net sales were $378.41 billion, compared to $337.86 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $72.87 billion, compared to $83.49 billion. Operating income increased to $112.86 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.92 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $72.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
