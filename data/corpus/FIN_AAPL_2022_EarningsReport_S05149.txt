Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $399.74 billion, compared to $370.13 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $97.70 billion, compared to $67.74 billion. Operating income increased to $118.83 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.57 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $49.18 billion at the end of the reporting period.
