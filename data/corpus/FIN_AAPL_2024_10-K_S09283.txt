Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $458.18 billion, compared to $424.24 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $81.68 billion, compared to $101.93 billion. Operating income increased to $113.61 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.10 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $157.51 billion at the end of the reporting period.
