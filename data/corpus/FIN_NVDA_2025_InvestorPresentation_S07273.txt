Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $261.43 billion, compared to $163.39 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $62.64 billion, compared to $35.76 billion. Operating income increased to $78.18 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $99.39 billion at the end of the reporting period.
