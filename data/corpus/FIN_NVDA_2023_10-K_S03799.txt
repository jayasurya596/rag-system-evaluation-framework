Operating income increased to $21.70 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $10.00 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure.
