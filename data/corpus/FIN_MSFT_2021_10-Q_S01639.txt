Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $198.91 billion, compared to $172.96 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $40.59 billion, compared to $32.53 billion. Operating income increased to $40.60 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $19.97 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $72.68 billion at the end of the reporting period.
