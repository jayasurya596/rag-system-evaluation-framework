For the fiscal year, total net sales were $31.21 billion, compared to $26.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.29 billion, compared to $5.07 billion. Operating income increased to $9.35 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.54 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $3.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
