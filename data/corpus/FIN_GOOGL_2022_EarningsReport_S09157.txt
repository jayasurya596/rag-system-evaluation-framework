Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $292.62 billion, compared to $261.27 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $55.27 billion, compared to $51.60 billion. Operating income increased to $63.15 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.94 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $76.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
