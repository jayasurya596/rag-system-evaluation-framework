Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $493.29 billion, compared to $456.75 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $101.53 billion, compared to $73.64 billion. Operating income increased to $106.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.60 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $175.34 billion at the end of the reporting period.
