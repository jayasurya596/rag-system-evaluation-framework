For the fiscal year, total net sales were $321.57 billion, compared to $279.63 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $76.36 billion, compared to $45.36 billion. Operating income increased to $69.31 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $78.97 billion at the end of the reporting period.
