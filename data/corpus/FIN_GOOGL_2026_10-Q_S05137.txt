For the fiscal year, total net sales were $452.98 billion, compared to $404.44 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $73.60 billion, compared to $90.86 billion. Operating income increased to $105.20 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.41 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $149.44 billion at the end of the reporting period.
