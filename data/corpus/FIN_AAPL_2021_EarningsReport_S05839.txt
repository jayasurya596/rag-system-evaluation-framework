Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $361.73 billion, compared to $334.93 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $84.16 billion, compared to $70.56 billion. Operating income increased to $101.92 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.81 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $109.87 billion at the end of the reporting period.
