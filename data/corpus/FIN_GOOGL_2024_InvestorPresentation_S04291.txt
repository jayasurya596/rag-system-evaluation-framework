Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $367.68 billion, compared to $328.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $82.62 billion, compared to $67.23 billion. Operating income increased to $93.32 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.55 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $83.41 billion at the end of the reporting period.
