Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $400.60 billion, compared to $370.92 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $67.99 billion, compared to $86.76 billion. Operating income increased to $93.50 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.70 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $104.12 billion at the end of the reporting period.
