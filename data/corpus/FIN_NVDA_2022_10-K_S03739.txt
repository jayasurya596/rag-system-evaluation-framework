Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $64.68 billion, compared to $40.42 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $10.67 billion, compared to $10.07 billion. Operating income increased to $17.75 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $6.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $21.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
