Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $197.01 billion, compared to $171.32 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $34.30 billion, compared to $31.24 billion. Operating income increased to $45.34 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $20.02 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $29.58 billion at the end of the reporting period.
