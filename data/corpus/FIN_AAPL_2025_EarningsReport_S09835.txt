Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $521.58 billion, compared to $482.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $113.28 billion, compared to $115.39 billion. Operating income increased to $124.44 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $56.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $203.69 billion at the end of the reporting period.
