For the fiscal year, total net sales were $45.75 billion, compared to $38.13 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $8.70 billion, compared to $6.10 billion. Operating income increased to $10.17 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.23 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $14.86 billion at the end of the reporting period.
