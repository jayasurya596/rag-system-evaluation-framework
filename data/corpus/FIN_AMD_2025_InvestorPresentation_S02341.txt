Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $35.88 billion, compared to $29.90 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $8.87 billion, compared to $4.83 billion. Operating income increased to $8.29 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $5.96 billion at the end of the reporting period.
