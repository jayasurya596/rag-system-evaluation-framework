Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $449.73 billion, compared to $416.42 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $91.80 billion, compared to $64.97 billion. Operating income increased to $130.16 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $53.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $108.67 billion at the end of the reporting period.
