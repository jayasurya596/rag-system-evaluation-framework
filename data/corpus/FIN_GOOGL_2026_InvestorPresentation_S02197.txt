For the fiscal year, total net sales were $483.96 billion, compared to $432.11 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $84.91 billion, compared to $107.12 billion. Operating income increased to $129.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.19 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $165.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
