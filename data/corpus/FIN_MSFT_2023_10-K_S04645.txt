Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $264.73 billion, compared to $230.20 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $55.24 billion, compared to $42.98 billion. Operating income increased to $71.73 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.83 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $31.08 billion at the end of the reporting period.
