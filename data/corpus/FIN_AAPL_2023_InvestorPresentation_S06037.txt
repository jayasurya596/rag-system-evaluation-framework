Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $448.80 billion, compared to $415.55 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $88.17 billion, compared to $93.43 billion. Operating income increased to $93.03 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.17 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $128.42 billion at the end of the reporting period.
