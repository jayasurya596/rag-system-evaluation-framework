For the fiscal year, total net sales were $42.73 billion, compared to $35.61 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $9.26 billion, compared to $8.13 billion. Operating income increased to $10.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.30 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.38 billion at the end of the reporting period.
