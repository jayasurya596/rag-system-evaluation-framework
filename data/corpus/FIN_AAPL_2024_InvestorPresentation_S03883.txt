Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $471.85 billion, compared to $436.90 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $94.95 billion, compared to $87.90 billion. Operating income increased to $112.86 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $52.86 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $128.66 billion at the end of the reporting period.
