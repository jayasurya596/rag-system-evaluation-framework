Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $61.10 billion, compared to $38.19 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $12.61 billion, compared to $8.13 billion. Operating income increased to $16.01 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $6.89 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $17.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
