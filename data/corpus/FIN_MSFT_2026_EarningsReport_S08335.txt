For the fiscal year, total net sales were $432.31 billion, compared to $375.92 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $97.09 billion, compared to $73.25 billion. Operating income increased to $92.99 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.76 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $129.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
