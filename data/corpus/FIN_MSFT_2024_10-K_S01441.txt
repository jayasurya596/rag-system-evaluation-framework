Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $327.17 billion, compared to $284.50 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $56.06 billion, compared to $67.76 billion. Operating income increased to $91.30 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.88 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $57.85 billion at the end of the reporting period.
