Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $282.50 billion, compared to $245.65 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $57.68 billion, compared to $48.00 billion. Operating income increased to $78.42 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.32 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $90.43 billion at the end of the reporting period.
