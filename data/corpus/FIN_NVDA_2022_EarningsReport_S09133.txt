Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $66.63 billion, compared to $41.64 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $11.06 billion, compared to $6.89 billion. Operating income increased to $15.79 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $7.74 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $21.42 billion at the end of the reporting period.
