For the fiscal year, total net sales were $528.21 billion, compared to $489.09 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $128.26 billion, compared to $87.96 billion. Operating income increased to $114.60 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $60.64 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $129.31 billion at the end of the reporting period.
