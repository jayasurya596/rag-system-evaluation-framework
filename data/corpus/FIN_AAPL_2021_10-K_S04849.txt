Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $364.24 billion, compared to $337.26 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $67.16 billion, compared to $52.33 billion. Operating income increased to $97.03 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.46 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $94.93 billion at the end of the reporting period.
