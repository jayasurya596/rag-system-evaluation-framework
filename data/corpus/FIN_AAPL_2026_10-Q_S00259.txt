Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $564.71 billion, compared to $522.88 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $89.55 billion, compared to $96.41 billion. Operating income increased to $143.52 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $67.10 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $156.38 billion at the end of the reporting period.
