Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $370.96 billion, compared to $343.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $82.64 billion, compared to $59.76 billion. Operating income increased to $81.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.28 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $100.07 billion at the end of the reporting period.
