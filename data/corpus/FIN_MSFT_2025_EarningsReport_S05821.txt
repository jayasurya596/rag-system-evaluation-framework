For the fiscal year, total net sales were $372.91 billion, compared to $324.27 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $61.80 billion, compared to $56.96 billion. Operating income increased to $85.03 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.30 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $77.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
