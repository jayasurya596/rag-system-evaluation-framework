Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $265.02 billion, compared to $230.45 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $40.94 billion, compared to $57.47 billion. Operating income increased to $56.05 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.12 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $90.58 billion at the end of the reporting period.
