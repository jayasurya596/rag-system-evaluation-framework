Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $298.43 billion, compared to $266.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $55.12 billion, compared to $60.84 billion. Operating income increased to $73.64 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.45 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $35.98 billion at the end of the reporting period.
