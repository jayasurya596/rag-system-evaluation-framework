Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $260.94 billion, compared to $232.98 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $64.50 billion, compared to $54.49 billion. Operating income increased to $69.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.57 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $60.23 billion at the end of the reporting period.
