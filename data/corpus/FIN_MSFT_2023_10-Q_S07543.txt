Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $260.37 billion, compared to $226.41 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $56.99 billion, compared to $39.48 billion. Operating income increased to $65.52 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.86 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $68.76 billion at the end of the reporting period.
