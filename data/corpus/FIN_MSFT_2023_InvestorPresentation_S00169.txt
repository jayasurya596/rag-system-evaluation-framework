Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $267.60 billion, compared to $232.70 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $55.04 billion, compared to $48.67 billion. Operating income increased to $73.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $23.28 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $81.25 billion at the end of the reporting period.
