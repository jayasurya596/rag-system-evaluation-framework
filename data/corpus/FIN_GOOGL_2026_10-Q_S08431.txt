For the fiscal year, total net sales were $468.81 billion, compared to $418.58 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $90.13 billion, compared to $100.29 billion. Operating income increased to $115.91 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $92.04 billion at the end of the reporting period.
