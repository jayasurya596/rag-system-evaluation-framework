Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $229.22 billion, compared to $199.32 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $38.74 billion, compared to $48.03 billion. Operating income increased to $57.06 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.23 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $60.08 billion at the end of the reporting period.
