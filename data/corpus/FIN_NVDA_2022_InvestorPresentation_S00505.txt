Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $64.36 billion, compared to $40.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $14.22 billion, compared to $9.07 billion. Operating income increased to $13.75 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $5.63 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $13.81 billion at the end of the reporting period.
