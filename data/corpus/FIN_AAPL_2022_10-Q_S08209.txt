Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $405.56 billion, compared to $375.52 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $93.62 billion, compared to $71.13 billion. Operating income increased to $89.42 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.49 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $109.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
