For the fiscal year, total net sales were $39.59 billion, compared to $24.75 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $6.69 billion, compared to $4.01 billion. Operating income increased to $11.28 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.89 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $11.83 billion at the end of the reporting period.
