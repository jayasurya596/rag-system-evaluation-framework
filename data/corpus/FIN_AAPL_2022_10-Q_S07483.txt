Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $405.74 billion, compared to $375.68 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $83.12 billion, compared to $75.60 billion. Operating income increased to $84.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $41.55 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $102.07 billion at the end of the reporting period.
