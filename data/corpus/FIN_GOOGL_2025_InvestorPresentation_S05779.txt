For the fiscal year, total net sales were $440.45 billion, compared to $393.26 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $88.28 billion, compared to $63.82 billion. Operating income increased to $125.69 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $77.63 billion at the end of the reporting period.
