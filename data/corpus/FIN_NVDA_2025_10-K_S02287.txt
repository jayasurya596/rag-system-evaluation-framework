Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $270.46 billion, compared to $169.04 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $50.12 billion, compared to $32.46 billion. Operating income increased to $70.21 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.38 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $77.98 billion at the end of the reporting period.
