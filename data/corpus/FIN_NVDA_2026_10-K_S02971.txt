For the fiscal year, total net sales were $408.92 billion, compared to $255.58 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $76.43 billion, compared to $51.15 billion. Operating income increased to $104.50 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.17 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $49.12 billion at the end of the reporting period.
