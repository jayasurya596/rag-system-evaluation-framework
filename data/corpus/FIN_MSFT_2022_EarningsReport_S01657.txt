For the fiscal year, total net sales were $249.30 billion, compared to $216.79 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $42.11 billion, compared to $38.15 billion. Operating income increased to $71.00 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $20.06 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.08 billion at the end of the reporting period.
