For the fiscal year, total net sales were $412.52 billion, compared to $368.32 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $63.16 billion, compared to $65.11 billion. Operating income increased to $121.13 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.65 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $164.52 billion at the end of the reporting period.
