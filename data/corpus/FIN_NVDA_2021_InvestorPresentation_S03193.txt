Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $41.80 billion, compared to $26.13 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $7.99 billion, compared to $6.50 billion. Operating income increased to $9.09 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.58 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $15.00 billion at the end of the reporting period.
