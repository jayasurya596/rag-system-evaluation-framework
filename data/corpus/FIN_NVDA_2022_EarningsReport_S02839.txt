Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $65.62 billion, compared to $41.01 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $12.12 billion, compared to $8.15 billion. Operating income increased to $15.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $7.24 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $21.24 billion at the end of the reporting period.
