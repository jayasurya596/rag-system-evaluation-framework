Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $421.06 billion, compared to $389.87 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $90.66 billion, compared to $93.18 billion. Operating income increased to $84.25 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.51 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $84.02 billion at the end of the reporting period.
