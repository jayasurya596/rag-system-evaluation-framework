Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $456.47 billion, compared to $422.66 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $78.52 billion, compared to $100.13 billion. Operating income increased to $123.50 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.20 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $56.58 billion at the end of the reporting period.
