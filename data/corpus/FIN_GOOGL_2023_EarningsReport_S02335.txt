Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $344.76 billion, compared to $307.82 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $54.13 billion, compared to $50.44 billion. Operating income increased to $103.01 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.23 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $89.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
