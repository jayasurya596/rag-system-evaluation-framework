For the fiscal year, total net sales were $583.15 billion, compared to $539.95 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $96.84 billion, compared to $131.33 billion. Operating income increased to $173.73 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $56.03 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $101.10 billion at the end of the reporting period.
