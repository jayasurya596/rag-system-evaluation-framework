Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $105.58 billion, compared to $65.99 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $26.25 billion, compared to $13.85 billion. Operating income increased to $27.81 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $8.85 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $20.40 billion at the end of the reporting period.
