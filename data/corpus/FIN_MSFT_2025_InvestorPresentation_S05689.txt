Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $348.01 billion, compared to $302.62 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $77.43 billion, compared to $69.28 billion. Operating income increased to $78.50 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.43 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.57 billion at the end of the reporting period.
