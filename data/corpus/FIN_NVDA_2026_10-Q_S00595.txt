For the fiscal year, total net sales were $414.30 billion, compared to $258.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $92.70 billion, compared to $41.57 billion. Operating income increased to $104.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.33 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $138.20 billion at the end of the reporting period.
