Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $399.27 billion, compared to $369.70 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $69.04 billion, compared to $84.12 billion. Operating income increased to $116.58 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.42 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $44.54 billion at the end of the reporting period.
