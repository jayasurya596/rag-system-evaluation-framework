Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $386.20 billion, compared to $344.82 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $58.44 billion, compared to $75.60 billion. Operating income increased to $113.74 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.49 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $126.40 billion at the end of the reporting period.
