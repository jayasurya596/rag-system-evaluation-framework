Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $353.99 billion, compared to $316.06 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $80.61 billion, compared to $72.05 billion. Operating income increased to $71.06 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $40.24 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $56.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
