Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $321.97 billion, compared to $287.48 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $79.50 billion, compared to $54.65 billion. Operating income increased to $76.94 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.48 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $72.89 billion at the end of the reporting period.
