Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $370.63 billion, compared to $330.92 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $59.54 billion, compared to $71.08 billion. Operating income increased to $100.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.81 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $71.80 billion at the end of the reporting period.
