Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $508.76 billion, compared to $471.07 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $113.77 billion, compared to $83.30 billion. Operating income increased to $139.83 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.46 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $104.88 billion at the end of the reporting period.
