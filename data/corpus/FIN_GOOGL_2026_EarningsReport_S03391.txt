For the fiscal year, total net sales were $457.24 billion, compared to $408.25 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $113.04 billion, compared to $82.09 billion. Operating income increased to $106.05 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $41.38 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $113.73 billion at the end of the reporting period.
