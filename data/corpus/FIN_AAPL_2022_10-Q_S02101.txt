Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $427.14 billion, compared to $395.50 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $84.17 billion, compared to $96.28 billion. Operating income increased to $104.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.34 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $60.15 billion at the end of the reporting period.
