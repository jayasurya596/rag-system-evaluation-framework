Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $328.02 billion, compared to $285.24 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $65.16 billion, compared to $64.46 billion. Operating income increased to $67.40 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.54 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $62.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
