Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $390.81 billion, compared to $348.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $61.76 billion, compared to $63.25 billion. Operating income increased to $79.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.90 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $94.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
