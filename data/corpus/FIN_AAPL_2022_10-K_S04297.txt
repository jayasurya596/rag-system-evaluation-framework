Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $422.81 billion, compared to $391.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $86.88 billion, compared to $72.23 billion. Operating income increased to $114.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.11 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.43 billion at the end of the reporting period.
