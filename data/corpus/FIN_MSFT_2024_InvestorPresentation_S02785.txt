Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $302.30 billion, compared to $262.87 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $57.70 billion, compared to $60.87 billion. Operating income increased to $81.94 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.45 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $55.33 billion at the end of the reporting period.
