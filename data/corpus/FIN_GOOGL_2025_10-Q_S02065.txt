Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $408.40 billion, compared to $364.64 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $65.26 billion, compared to $74.75 billion. Operating income increased to $97.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $137.24 billion at the end of the reporting period.
