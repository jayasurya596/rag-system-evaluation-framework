Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $267.31 billion, compared to $238.67 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $66.61 billion, compared to $47.41 billion. Operating income increased to $69.08 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.99 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $39.67 billion at the end of the reporting period.
