Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $454.39 billion, compared to $420.73 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $100.87 billion, compared to $88.57 billion. Operating income increased to $112.82 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $53.96 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $126.76 billion at the end of the reporting period.
