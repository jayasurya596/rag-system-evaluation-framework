For the fiscal year, total net sales were $567.04 billion, compared to $525.04 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $90.50 billion, compared to $109.30 billion. Operating income increased to $139.20 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $58.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $141.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
