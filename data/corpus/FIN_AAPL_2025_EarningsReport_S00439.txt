Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $512.26 billion, compared to $474.31 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $107.09 billion, compared to $82.46 billion. Operating income increased to $114.93 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.24 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $125.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
