Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $25.45 billion, compared to $21.21 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $4.51 billion, compared to $4.46 billion. Operating income increased to $5.56 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.57 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $3.10 billion at the end of the reporting period.
