Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $207.10 billion, compared to $180.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $38.80 billion, compared to $32.08 billion. Operating income increased to $52.38 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $19.06 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $46.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
