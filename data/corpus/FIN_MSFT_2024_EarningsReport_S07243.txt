For the fiscal year, total net sales were $317.73 billion, compared to $276.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $50.39 billion, compared to $46.86 billion. Operating income increased to $68.62 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.60 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $95.32 billion at the end of the reporting period.
