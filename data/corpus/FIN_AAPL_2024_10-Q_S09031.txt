Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $460.59 billion, compared to $426.47 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $96.22 billion, compared to $93.80 billion. Operating income increased to $110.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.25 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $105.45 billion at the end of the reporting period.
