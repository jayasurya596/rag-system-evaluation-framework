Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $227.92 billion, compared to $198.19 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $48.60 billion, compared to $44.36 billion. Operating income increased to $62.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $18.24 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $34.45 billion at the end of the reporting period.
