Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $281.35 billion, compared to $251.20 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $59.38 billion, compared to $42.92 billion. Operating income increased to $77.63 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.74 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $70.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
