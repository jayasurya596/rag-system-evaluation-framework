Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $227.94 billion, compared to $198.21 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $53.32 billion, compared to $39.91 billion. Operating income increased to $67.30 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.09 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $48.72 billion at the end of the reporting period.
