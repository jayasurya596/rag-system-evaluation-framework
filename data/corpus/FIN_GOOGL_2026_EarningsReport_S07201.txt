For the fiscal year, total net sales were $482.11 billion, compared to $430.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $111.35 billion, compared to $99.55 billion. Operating income increased to $142.15 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.50 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $157.54 billion at the end of the reporting period.
