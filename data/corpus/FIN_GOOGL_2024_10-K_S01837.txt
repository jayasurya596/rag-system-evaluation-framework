Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $358.89 billion, compared to $320.44 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $73.88 billion, compared to $49.74 billion. Operating income increased to $85.36 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.21 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $102.04 billion at the end of the reporting period.
