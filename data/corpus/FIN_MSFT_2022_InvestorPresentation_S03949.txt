Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $245.86 billion, compared to $213.79 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $40.02 billion, compared to $48.24 billion. Operating income increased to $55.20 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $26.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $27.82 billion at the end of the reporting period.
