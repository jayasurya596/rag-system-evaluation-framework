Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $303.70 billion, compared to $271.16 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $57.40 billion, compared to $43.97 billion. Operating income increased to $65.29 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $105.84 billion at the end of the reporting period.
