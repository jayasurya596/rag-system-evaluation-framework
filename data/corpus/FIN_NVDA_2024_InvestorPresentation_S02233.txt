Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $169.28 billion, compared to $105.80 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $27.17 billion, compared to $16.41 billion. Operating income increased to $49.99 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $17.44 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $36.85 billion at the end of the reporting period.
