Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $389.10 billion, compared to $360.28 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $80.58 billion, compared to $89.89 billion. Operating income increased to $101.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $39.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $131.56 billion at the end of the reporting period.
