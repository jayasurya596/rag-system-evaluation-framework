For the fiscal year, total net sales were $437.44 billion, compared to $273.40 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $66.94 billion, compared to $57.37 billion. Operating income increased to $113.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $156.80 billion at the end of the reporting period.
