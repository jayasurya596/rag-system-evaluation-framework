Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $276.99 billion, compared to $240.86 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $67.84 billion, compared to $57.09 billion. Operating income increased to $80.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.43 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $88.68 billion at the end of the reporting period.
