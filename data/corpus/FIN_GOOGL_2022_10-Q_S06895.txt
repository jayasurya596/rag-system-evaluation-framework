Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $305.41 billion, compared to $272.69 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $65.58 billion, compared to $45.86 billion. Operating income increased to $75.69 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.28 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $98.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
