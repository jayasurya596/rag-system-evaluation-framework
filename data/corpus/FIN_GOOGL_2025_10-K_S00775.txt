Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $408.40 billion, compared to $364.64 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $80.45 billion, compared to $78.04 billion. Operating income increased to $94.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $114.48 billion at the end of the reporting period.
