For the fiscal year, total net sales were $362.00 billion, compared to $314.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $60.56 billion, compared to $73.56 billion. Operating income increased to $82.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.20 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $127.39 billion at the end of the reporting period.
