For the fiscal year, total net sales were $475.97 billion, compared to $424.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $97.70 billion, compared to $79.82 billion. Operating income increased to $114.65 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.59 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $145.22 billion at the end of the reporting period.
