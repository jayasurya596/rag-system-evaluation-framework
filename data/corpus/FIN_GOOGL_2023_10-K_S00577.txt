Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $320.59 billion, compared to $286.24 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $68.30 billion, compared to $63.35 billion. Operating income increased to $95.52 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.53 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $102.95 billion at the end of the reporting period.
