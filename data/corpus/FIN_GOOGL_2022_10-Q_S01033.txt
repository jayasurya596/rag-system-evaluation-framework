Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $286.38 billion, compared to $255.69 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $49.94 billion, compared to $38.89 billion. Operating income increased to $74.81 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.51 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $64.25 billion at the end of the reporting period.
