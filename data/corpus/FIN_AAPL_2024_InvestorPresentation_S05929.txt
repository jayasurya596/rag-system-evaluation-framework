Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $488.52 billion, compared to $452.33 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $85.30 billion, compared to $86.33 billion. Operating income increased to $124.66 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $51.01 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $129.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
