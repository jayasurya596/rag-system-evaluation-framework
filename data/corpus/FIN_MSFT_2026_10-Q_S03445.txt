For the fiscal year, total net sales were $431.71 billion, compared to $375.40 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $81.61 billion, compared to $87.38 billion. Operating income increased to $105.89 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.45 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $152.61 billion at the end of the reporting period.
