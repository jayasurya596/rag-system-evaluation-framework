Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $318.73 billion, compared to $277.16 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $51.62 billion, compared to $42.25 billion. Operating income increased to $78.10 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $117.68 billion at the end of the reporting period.
