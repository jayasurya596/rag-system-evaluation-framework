Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $210.34 billion, compared to $182.91 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $39.68 billion, compared to $41.49 billion. Operating income increased to $48.89 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.96 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.07 billion at the end of the reporting period.
