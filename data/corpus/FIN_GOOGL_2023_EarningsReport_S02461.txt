Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $325.64 billion, compared to $290.75 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $52.66 billion, compared to $60.25 billion. Operating income increased to $87.15 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $36.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
