For the fiscal year, total net sales were $486.89 billion, compared to $434.72 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $96.87 billion, compared to $82.79 billion. Operating income increased to $110.92 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure.
