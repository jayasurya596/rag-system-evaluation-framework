Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $308.21 billion, compared to $275.19 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $47.26 billion, compared to $53.96 billion. Operating income increased to $85.40 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.08 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $121.63 billion at the end of the reporting period.
