For the fiscal year, total net sales were $330.82 billion, compared to $295.38 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $52.76 billion, compared to $69.78 billion. Operating income increased to $95.81 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.51 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $58.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
