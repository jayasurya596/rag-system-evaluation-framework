Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $229.38 billion, compared to $199.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $37.54 billion, compared to $30.66 billion. Operating income increased to $68.61 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $20.48 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $90.19 billion at the end of the reporting period.
