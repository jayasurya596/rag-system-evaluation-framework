Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $402.91 billion, compared to $373.07 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $84.67 billion, compared to $90.58 billion. Operating income increased to $89.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $36.49 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $122.43 billion at the end of the reporting period.
