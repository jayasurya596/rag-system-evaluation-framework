Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $483.30 billion, compared to $447.50 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $101.45 billion, compared to $107.68 billion. Operating income increased to $131.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $135.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
