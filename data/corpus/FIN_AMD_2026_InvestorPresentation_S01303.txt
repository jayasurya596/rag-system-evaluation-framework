Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $45.23 billion, compared to $37.69 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $9.91 billion, compared to $7.57 billion. Operating income increased to $10.37 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.85 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $16.50 billion at the end of the reporting period.
