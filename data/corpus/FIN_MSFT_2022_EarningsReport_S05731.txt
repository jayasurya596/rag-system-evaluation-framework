Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $237.05 billion, compared to $206.13 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $41.35 billion, compared to $39.99 billion. Operating income increased to $56.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.21 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $42.11 billion at the end of the reporting period.
