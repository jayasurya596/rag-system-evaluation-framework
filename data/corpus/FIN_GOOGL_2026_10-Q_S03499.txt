Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $466.32 billion, compared to $416.36 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $75.59 billion, compared to $96.80 billion. Operating income increased to $108.35 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.82 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $177.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
