Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $323.47 billion, compared to $288.81 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $61.21 billion, compared to $58.94 billion. Operating income increased to $72.19 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.09 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $86.23 billion at the end of the reporting period.
