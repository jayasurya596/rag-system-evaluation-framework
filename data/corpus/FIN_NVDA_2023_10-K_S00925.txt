Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $102.05 billion, compared to $63.78 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $23.84 billion, compared to $10.12 billion. Operating income increased to $29.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $8.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $38.31 billion at the end of the reporting period.
