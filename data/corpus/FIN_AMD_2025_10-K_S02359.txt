Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $35.94 billion, compared to $29.95 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $5.74 billion, compared to $5.57 billion. Operating income increased to $8.33 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.90 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.17 billion at the end of the reporting period.
