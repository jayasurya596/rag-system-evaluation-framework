Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $296.97 billion, compared to $265.15 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $71.01 billion, compared to $66.03 billion. Operating income increased to $88.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.59 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $51.90 billion at the end of the reporting period.
