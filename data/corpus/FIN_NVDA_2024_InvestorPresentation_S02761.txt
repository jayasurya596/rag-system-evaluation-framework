Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $156.99 billion, compared to $98.12 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $36.19 billion, compared to $24.33 billion. Operating income increased to $39.28 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $15.49 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $16.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
