Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $550.40 billion, compared to $509.63 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $115.42 billion, compared to $92.26 billion. Operating income increased to $162.03 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $58.44 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $144.66 billion at the end of the reporting period.
