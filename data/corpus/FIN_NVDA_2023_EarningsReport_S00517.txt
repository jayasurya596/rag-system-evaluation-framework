Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $98.92 billion, compared to $61.83 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $19.79 billion, compared to $13.78 billion. Operating income increased to $22.18 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $10.17 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $12.83 billion at the end of the reporting period.
