Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $99.80 billion, compared to $62.37 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $24.78 billion, compared to $11.49 billion. Operating income increased to $24.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $10.38 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.46 billion at the end of the reporting period.
