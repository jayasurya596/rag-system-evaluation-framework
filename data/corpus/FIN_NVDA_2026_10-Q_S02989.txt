For the fiscal year, total net sales were $421.17 billion, compared to $263.23 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $73.84 billion, compared to $60.36 billion. Operating income increased to $91.61 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.31 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $55.20 billion at the end of the reporting period.
