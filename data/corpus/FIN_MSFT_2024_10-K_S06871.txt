Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $300.84 billion, compared to $261.60 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $58.04 billion, compared to $59.94 billion. Operating income increased to $66.10 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.72 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $106.80 billion at the end of the reporting period.
