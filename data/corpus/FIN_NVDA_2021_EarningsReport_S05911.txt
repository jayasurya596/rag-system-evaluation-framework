For the fiscal year, total net sales were $41.69 billion, compared to $26.06 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $10.27 billion, compared to $6.09 billion. Operating income increased to $9.60 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.02 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $8.78 billion at the end of the reporting period.
