For the fiscal year, total net sales were $424.32 billion, compared to $378.86 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $100.25 billion, compared to $71.50 billion. Operating income increased to $94.06 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.97 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $64.61 billion at the end of the reporting period.
