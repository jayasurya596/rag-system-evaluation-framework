Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $455.62 billion, compared to $421.87 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $80.34 billion, compared to $85.42 billion. Operating income increased to $106.22 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.95 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $92.76 billion at the end of the reporting period.
