For the fiscal year, total net sales were $563.39 billion, compared to $521.65 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $135.42 billion, compared to $89.17 billion. Operating income increased to $150.17 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $52.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $167.04 billion at the end of the reporting period.
