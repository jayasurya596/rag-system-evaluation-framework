Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $18.49 billion, compared to $15.41 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $3.29 billion, compared to $2.84 billion. Operating income increased to $5.54 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.41 billion at the end of the reporting period.
