Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $159.14 billion, compared to $99.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $35.05 billion, compared to $19.92 billion. Operating income increased to $46.95 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $13.65 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $51.67 billion at the end of the reporting period.
