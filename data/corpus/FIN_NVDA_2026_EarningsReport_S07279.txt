For the fiscal year, total net sales were $431.91 billion, compared to $269.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $98.12 billion, compared to $56.54 billion. Operating income increased to $120.95 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.90 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $122.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
