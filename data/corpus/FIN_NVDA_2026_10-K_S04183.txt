Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $422.54 billion, compared to $264.09 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $100.09 billion, compared to $58.86 billion. Operating income increased to $119.51 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.69 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $82.43 billion at the end of the reporting period.
