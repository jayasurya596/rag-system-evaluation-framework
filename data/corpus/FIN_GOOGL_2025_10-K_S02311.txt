Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $433.32 billion, compared to $386.89 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $99.33 billion, compared to $89.24 billion. Operating income increased to $125.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $81.80 billion at the end of the reporting period.
