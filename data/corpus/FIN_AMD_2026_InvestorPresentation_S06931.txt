For the fiscal year, total net sales were $44.68 billion, compared to $37.23 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.72 billion, compared to $6.92 billion. Operating income increased to $13.32 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $9.54 billion at the end of the reporting period.
