Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $35.52 billion, compared to $29.60 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.96 billion, compared to $5.81 billion. Operating income increased to $9.90 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.04 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.03 billion at the end of the reporting period.
