Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $385.42 billion, compared to $344.12 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $74.85 billion, compared to $64.57 billion. Operating income increased to $103.68 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.89 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $152.64 billion at the end of the reporting period.
