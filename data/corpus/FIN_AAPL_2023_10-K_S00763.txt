Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $445.45 billion, compared to $412.45 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $104.53 billion, compared to $63.02 billion. Operating income increased to $101.66 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $45.29 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $53.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
