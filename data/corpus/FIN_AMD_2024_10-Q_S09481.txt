Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $31.60 billion, compared to $26.34 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.67 billion, compared to $4.05 billion. Operating income increased to $7.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.57 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.91 billion at the end of the reporting period.
