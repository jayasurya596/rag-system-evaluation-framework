For the fiscal year, total net sales were $411.48 billion, compared to $257.18 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $100.22 billion, compared to $47.59 billion. Operating income increased to $83.35 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $108.17 billion at the end of the reporting period.
