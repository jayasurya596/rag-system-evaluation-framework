Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $274.72 billion, compared to $245.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $56.12 billion, compared to $48.88 billion. Operating income increased to $60.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $29.08 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $48.34 billion at the end of the reporting period.
