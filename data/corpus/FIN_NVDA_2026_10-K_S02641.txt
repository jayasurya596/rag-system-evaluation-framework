For the fiscal year, total net sales were $424.78 billion, compared to $265.49 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $75.45 billion, compared to $53.00 billion. Operating income increased to $124.41 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $47.90 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $49.60 billion at the end of the reporting period.
