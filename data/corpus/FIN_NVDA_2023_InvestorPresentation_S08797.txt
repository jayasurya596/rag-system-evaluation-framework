Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $105.31 billion, compared to $65.82 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $18.55 billion, compared to $10.68 billion. Operating income increased to $28.43 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $8.89 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $25.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
