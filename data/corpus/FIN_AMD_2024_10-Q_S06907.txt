Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $31.26 billion, compared to $26.05 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $5.66 billion, compared to $6.38 billion. Operating income increased to $7.86 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.55 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $12.01 billion at the end of the reporting period.
