Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $339.90 billion, compared to $303.48 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $74.86 billion, compared to $75.26 billion. Operating income increased to $83.60 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $120.40 billion at the end of the reporting period.
