Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $359.12 billion, compared to $332.52 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $77.59 billion, compared to $60.57 billion. Operating income increased to $82.07 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $32.75 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $101.26 billion at the end of the reporting period.
