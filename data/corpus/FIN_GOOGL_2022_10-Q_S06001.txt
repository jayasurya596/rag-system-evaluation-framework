Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $296.76 billion, compared to $264.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $72.55 billion, compared to $45.29 billion. Operating income increased to $61.45 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.05 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $105.37 billion at the end of the reporting period.
