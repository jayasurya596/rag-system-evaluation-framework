Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $333.22 billion, compared to $297.52 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $75.69 billion, compared to $64.41 billion. Operating income increased to $67.34 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $39.95 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $82.99 billion at the end of the reporting period.
