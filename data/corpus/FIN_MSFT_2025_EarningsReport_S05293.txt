For the fiscal year, total net sales were $367.47 billion, compared to $319.53 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $74.45 billion, compared to $54.00 billion. Operating income increased to $74.02 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.75 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.88 billion at the end of the reporting period.
