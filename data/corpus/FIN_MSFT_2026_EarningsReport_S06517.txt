For the fiscal year, total net sales were $425.57 billion, compared to $370.06 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $69.68 billion, compared to $76.81 billion. Operating income increased to $96.67 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $50.03 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $136.60 billion at the end of the reporting period.
