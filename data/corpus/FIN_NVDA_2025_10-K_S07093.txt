Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $258.28 billion, compared to $161.43 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $61.98 billion, compared to $29.70 billion. Operating income increased to $77.27 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $21.60 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $84.21 billion at the end of the reporting period.
