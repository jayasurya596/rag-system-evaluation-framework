For the fiscal year, total net sales were $30.85 billion, compared to $25.71 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.34 billion, compared to $4.41 billion. Operating income increased to $6.70 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.75 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $8.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
