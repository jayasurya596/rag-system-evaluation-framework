Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $460.54 billion, compared to $426.43 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $98.17 billion, compared to $70.64 billion. Operating income increased to $135.68 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $43.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $86.21 billion at the end of the reporting period.
