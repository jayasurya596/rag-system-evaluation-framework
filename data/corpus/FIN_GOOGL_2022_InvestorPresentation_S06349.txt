For the fiscal year, total net sales were $305.21 billion, compared to $272.51 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $70.76 billion, compared to $50.05 billion. Operating income increased to $72.26 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $31.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $103.57 billion at the end of the reporting period.
