For the fiscal year, total net sales were $36.28 billion, compared to $30.23 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $7.57 billion, compared to $6.53 billion. Operating income increased to $10.39 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.16 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $6.42 billion at the end of the reporting period.
