Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $105.19 billion, compared to $65.74 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $22.09 billion, compared to $13.01 billion. Operating income increased to $25.35 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $8.62 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $30.35 billion at the end of the reporting period.
