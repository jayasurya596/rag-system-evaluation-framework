For the fiscal year, total net sales were $414.91 billion, compared to $360.79 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $68.48 billion, compared to $69.28 billion. Operating income increased to $99.22 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $44.38 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $149.47 billion at the end of the reporting period.
