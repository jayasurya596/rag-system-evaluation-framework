Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $361.43 billion, compared to $314.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $79.05 billion, compared to $67.19 billion. Operating income increased to $94.73 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $141.09 billion at the end of the reporting period.
