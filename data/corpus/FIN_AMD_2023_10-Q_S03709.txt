Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $27.11 billion, compared to $22.59 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $5.43 billion, compared to $5.18 billion. Operating income increased to $7.14 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $8.36 billion at the end of the reporting period.
