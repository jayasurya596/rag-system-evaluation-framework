Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $404.69 billion, compared to $361.33 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $93.05 billion, compared to $79.84 billion. Operating income increased to $92.20 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $37.26 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $132.79 billion at the end of the reporting period.
