Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $32.43 billion, compared to $27.03 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $6.27 billion, compared to $5.48 billion. Operating income increased to $8.64 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $3.06 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.10 billion at the end of the reporting period.
