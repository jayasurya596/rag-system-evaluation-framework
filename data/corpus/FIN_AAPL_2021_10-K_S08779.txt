Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $374.81 billion, compared to $347.05 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $85.32 billion, compared to $73.20 billion. Operating income increased to $102.98 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $125.74 billion at the end of the reporting period.
