Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $307.49 billion, compared to $274.54 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $51.98 billion, compared to $64.84 billion. Operating income increased to $85.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.14 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $76.33 billion at the end of the reporting period.
