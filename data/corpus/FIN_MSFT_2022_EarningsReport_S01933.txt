Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $227.93 billion, compared to $198.20 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $35.59 billion, compared to $39.20 billion. Operating income increased to $68.02 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $19.11 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $80.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
