Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $104.62 billion, compared to $65.39 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $25.68 billion, compared to $15.88 billion. Operating income increased to $24.68 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $10.49 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $22.38 billion at the end of the reporting period.
