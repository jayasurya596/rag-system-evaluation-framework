Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $453.71 billion, compared to $420.11 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $110.69 billion, compared to $105.02 billion. Operating income increased to $101.15 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.06 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $125.67 billion at the end of the reporting period.
