For the fiscal year, total net sales were $411.52 billion, compared to $257.20 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $96.19 billion, compared to $42.47 billion. Operating income increased to $92.38 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $42.74 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $73.06 billion at the end of the reporting period.
