Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $266.03 billion, compared to $231.33 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $59.93 billion, compared to $49.28 billion. Operating income increased to $68.58 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $28.09 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $67.05 billion at the end of the reporting period.
