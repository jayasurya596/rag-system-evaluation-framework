Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $460.02 billion, compared to $425.95 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $100.99 billion, compared to $100.41 billion. Operating income increased to $105.53 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $46.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $102.21 billion at the end of the reporting period.
