For the fiscal year, total net sales were $275.38 billion, compared to $239.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $66.69 billion, compared to $51.77 billion. Operating income increased to $72.77 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.00 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $46.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
