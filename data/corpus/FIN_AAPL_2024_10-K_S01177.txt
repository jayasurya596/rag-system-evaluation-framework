Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $488.48 billion, compared to $452.30 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $94.44 billion, compared to $91.09 billion. Operating income increased to $130.24 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $49.79 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $158.60 billion at the end of the reporting period.
