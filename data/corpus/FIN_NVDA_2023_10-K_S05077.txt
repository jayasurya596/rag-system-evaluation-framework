Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $100.40 billion, compared to $62.75 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $18.90 billion, compared to $14.04 billion. Operating income increased to $28.76 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $11.37 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $18.71 billion at the end of the reporting period.
