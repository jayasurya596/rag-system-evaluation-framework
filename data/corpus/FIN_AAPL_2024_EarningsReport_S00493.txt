Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $498.53 billion, compared to $461.60 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $101.77 billion, compared to $75.33 billion. Operating income increased to $116.99 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.73 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $146.27 billion at the end of the reporting period.
