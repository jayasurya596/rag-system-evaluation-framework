Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $158.72 billion, compared to $99.20 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $39.01 billion, compared to $18.17 billion. Operating income increased to $33.56 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $18.59 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $59.10 billion at the end of the reporting period.
