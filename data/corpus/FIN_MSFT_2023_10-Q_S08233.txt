Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $275.01 billion, compared to $239.14 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $58.96 billion, compared to $36.32 billion. Operating income increased to $80.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $22.44 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $44.83 billion at the end of the reporting period.
