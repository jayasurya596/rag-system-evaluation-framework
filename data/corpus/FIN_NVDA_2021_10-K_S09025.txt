Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $38.87 billion, compared to $24.30 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $7.36 billion, compared to $4.67 billion. Operating income increased to $8.31 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $4.15 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $10.81 billion at the end of the reporting period.
