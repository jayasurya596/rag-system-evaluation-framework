Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $367.05 billion, compared to $327.72 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $55.19 billion, compared to $56.47 billion. Operating income increased to $81.86 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $38.52 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $63.02 billion at the end of the reporting period.
