Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $417.34 billion, compared to $386.43 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $90.22 billion, compared to $93.73 billion. Operating income increased to $115.18 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $48.95 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $66.52 billion at the end of the reporting period.
