Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $278.84 billion, compared to $248.97 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $66.48 billion, compared to $45.80 billion. Operating income increased to $58.86 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $33.21 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $37.75 billion at the end of the reporting period.
