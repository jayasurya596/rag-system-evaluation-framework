Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $434.93 billion, compared to $402.71 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 8.0%. Net income was $98.45 billion, compared to $82.26 billion. Operating income increased to $123.58 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $35.14 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $131.67 billion at the end of the reporting period.
