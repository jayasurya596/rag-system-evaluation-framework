Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $216.30 billion, compared to $188.08 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $43.98 billion, compared to $44.45 billion. Operating income increased to $50.58 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $19.10 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $53.64 billion at the end of the reporting period.
