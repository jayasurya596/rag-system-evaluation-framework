Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $310.76 billion, compared to $270.22 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $56.95 billion, compared to $63.42 billion. Operating income increased to $70.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $34.36 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $67.44 billion at the end of the reporting period.
