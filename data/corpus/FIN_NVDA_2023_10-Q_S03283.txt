Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $101.27 billion, compared to $63.29 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 60.0%. Net income was $21.57 billion, compared to $12.60 billion. Operating income increased to $29.87 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $9.18 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $32.80 billion at the end of the reporting period.
