Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $300.77 billion, compared to $268.54 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $47.83 billion, compared to $46.53 billion. Operating income increased to $66.73 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.40 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $30.14 billion at the end of the reporting period.
