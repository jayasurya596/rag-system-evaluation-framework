For the fiscal year, total net sales were $297.32 billion, compared to $265.46 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $55.99 billion, compared to $55.72 billion. Operating income increased to $62.46 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $25.88 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $101.34 billion at the end of the reporting period.
