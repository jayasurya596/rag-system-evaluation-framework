Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $333.93 billion, compared to $298.16 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $62.55 billion, compared to $69.81 billion. Operating income increased to $87.55 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $27.10 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $35.98 billion at the end of the reporting period.
