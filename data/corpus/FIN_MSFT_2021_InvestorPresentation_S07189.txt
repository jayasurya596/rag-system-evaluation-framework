Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $200.74 billion, compared to $174.55 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 15.0%. Net income was $32.31 billion, compared to $29.36 billion. Operating income increased to $53.78 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $16.49 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $52.97 billion at the end of the reporting period.
