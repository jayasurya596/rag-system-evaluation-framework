Section: Financial Position and MD&A
========================================

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $32.64 billion, compared to $27.20 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 20.0%. Net income was $5.12 billion, compared to $5.56 billion. Operating income increased to $7.02 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $2.65 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $4.16 billion at the end of the reporting period.
