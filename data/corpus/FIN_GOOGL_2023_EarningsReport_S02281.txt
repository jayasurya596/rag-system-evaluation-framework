Section: Financial Position and MD&A

For the fiscal year, total net sales were $337.05 billion, compared to $300.94 billion in the prior year, representing a growth of 12.0%. Net income was $54.65 billion, compared to $73.18 billion. Operating income increased to $81.98 billion due to improved efficiencies in our global supply chain and strong demand for our premium tier products. Research and Development expenses were $30.07 billion, reflecting our persistent commitment to investing in artificial intelligence, custom chip design, and cloud infrastructure. Cash and cash equivalents stood at $130.65 billion at the end of the reporting period.
